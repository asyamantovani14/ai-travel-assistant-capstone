Day 1-2: Tokyo
- Drive from Tokyo to Japan (500km, 6 hours)
- Stay at Japan Inn or Japan Grand Hotel (both are pet-friendly)
- Explore the bustling city with technology and culture

Day 3-5: Kyoto
- Drive from Tokyo to Kyoto (approximately 8 hours)
- Stay at a pet-friendly hotel like Japan Stay & Go
- Explore famous temples, shrines, and traditional tea houses in Kyoto

Make sure to plan your itinerary carefully to make the most of your 5-day trip to Japan, enjoying the unique experiences in both Tokyo and Kyoto.
