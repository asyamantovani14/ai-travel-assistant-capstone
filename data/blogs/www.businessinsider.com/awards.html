Business Insider's work has won global recognition and some of the highest honors in journalism for its investigative and original reporting, its creative use of data and visual aids, and its deeply researched, evocative videos. Highlights include:

  • Two 2025 News & Documentary Emmys for The Man Who Feeds Gaza's Children and The True Cost of Mining Electric Car Battery Metals
  • A 2024 National Magazine Award in Video for How dogs are trained to attack US prisoners by Hannah Beckler, Olivia Nemec, Robert Leslie, Noah Lewis and Erica Berenstein
  • A 2024 Excellence in Financial Journalism Award for a package on AI's impact on the workforce by Aki Ito
  • A 2024 Hillman Award in Newspaper Journalism for Patrol dogs are terrorizing and mauling prisoners inside the United States and How dogs are trained to attack US prisoners by Hannah Beckler
  • A 2022 Pulitzer Award in Illustrated Reporting and Commentary for How I escaped a Chinese internment camp by Fahmida Azim, Anthony Del Col, Josh Adams, and Walt Hickey
