48 best gifts for aunts, from luxe silk PJs to a global coffee subscription

Whether you see your aunt as your cool older sibling, parental figure, or role model, she plays an important role in your life. As such, your aunt deserves a gift that's as special as she is.  

This year, give your favorite aunt something thoughtful that really shows you care. Not sure where to start? We've got you covered. Whether it's something to celebrate a milestone birthday or a small stocking stuffer, we've come up with plenty of gifts your aunt (or whatever nickname she goes by!) will appreciate. 

This gift guide includes a sponsored product that has been suggested by Erno Laszlo. It also meets our editorial criteria in terms of quality and value.

Check price at Amazon
Check price at Nordstrom
Check price at Click and Grow

This way, she'll always have access to fresh ingredients — be they basil, thyme, sage, parsley, cilantro, cherry tomatoes, rosemary, peppers, flowers, or even strawberries.

Check price at Mark & Graham

A lottery card that wins money for charities
