The 37 best gift baskets and bundles to give this holiday season

If the way to their heart is through their stomach, you're in the right place. While there are many ways to approach finding the right holiday gift for your favorite foodie, we think there's no better way than gifting them something straight-up delicious. 

That's why we curated an extensive list of the best gift baskets they can dig into immediately. Whether you're looking to treat their tastebuds to a culinary adventure or want to help them stock up on their favorite pantry essentials, this guide has something for every type of food-loving friend or family member.

A tin of arguably the best chocolate chip cookies ever

Levain Signature Cookie Assortment

Check price at Levain Bakery

Levain arguably makes the best cookies in New York City, but the good news is anyone across the country can enjoy the chunky, chewy cookies thanks to Levain's nationwide shipping. The Signature Cookie Assortment is totally giftable, with eight cookies in a keepsake tin box. There are a variety of flavor assortment options to choose from, including the Signature Assortment, Vegan & Gluten-free Chocolate Chip Walnut, Chocolate Lovers, Fall Chocolate Chunk, and more, so you can be sure to get a combination their tastebuds will love most.

A taste of New York at home

"Best of Magnolia Bakery" Sampler Pack

Check price at Magnolia Bakery

Gift a taste of this famous Big Apple bakery with this sweet gift. Magnolia Bakery, a New York City institution, has put together this selection of cupcakes and banana pudding that ships anywhere across the country.

A feast from the best restaurants in New York City

Goldbelly "Taste of New York" Weekender Box

Check price at Goldbelly

The box from Goldbelly features iconic New York Foods from some of the city's most beloved restaurants, including pizza from John's of Bleeker Street, pastrami from Eli Zabar, cheesecake from Junior's, bagels from Zucker's, and black-and-white cookies from William Greenberg Desserts.

Instant coffee meets pour-over sampler

Copper Cow Coffee Pour Over Coffee Variety Bundle and Mug

Check price at Copper Cow Coffee

Copper Cow specializes in a "portable pour-over," which brews a strong and bold cup with the convenience of instant coffee. This pack has three flavors made with natural spices. The bundle also includes a glass mug printed with standard coffee measurements, such as the size of an espresso shot and the ratio of a milk latte.

A basket fit for your favorite home cook

Harry & David Italian Inspirations Basket

Check price at Harry & David

With pasta, olives, salami, and other ingredients, this massive basket will be an appreciated addition to any kitchen.

A gourmet and trendy selection of tinned fish

Fishwife Party Pack

Check price at Fishwife

Tinned fish may seem like a strange gift, but Fishwife has made it cool. The party pack includes five flavors of premium preserved trout, tuna, sardines, anchovies, and smoked salmon. Whether you're stocking a charcuterie board or expanding your pantry palate, Fishwife is great niche gift.

A luxury box of chocolates

Check price at Vosges Haut-Chocolat

One of the best chocolate brands for gifting is Vosges Chocolate. Their gift boxes and towers are impeccably wrapped and feature luxurious sweet treats. This tower comes with three boxes of classics, sure to appeal to any chocolate fan: caramel marshmallows, caramel toffee, and peanut butter bonbons.

Five pints of the internet's favorite ice cream

Salt & Straw The Best Sellers Pack: Classics

Check price at Salt & Straw

It's usually hard to gift ice cream without it showing up at the door as a melted mess. Thankfully, Salt & Straw packs all its gifts with dry ice. Your recipient can enjoy this pack of five ice cream pack in the brand's best-selling flavors: Sea Salt with Caramel Ribbons; Double Fold Vanilla; Freckled Mint Chocolate Chip; Chocolate Gooey Brownie; and Salted, Malted, Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough

Non-alcoholic spirits for sober parties

Check price at Free Spirits

When we tried the best non-alcoholic whiskeys, Free Spirits Bourbon was the clear winner. Now, you can get a gift set of non-alcoholic spirits, including tequila, bourbon, and gin. Whether you're mixing up mocktails or sipping neat, having a sober-friendly drink on hand is a party essential.

A meat lover's dream

Omaha Steaks Custom Assortment with Free Dessert

Check price at Omaha Steaks

Omaha Steaks has been in business for over a century, and anyone who enjoys a good cut of meat will appreciate this lavish gift. The Custom Assortment package allows the gifter to pick and choose from various cuts of meat, making the gift even more personalized.

A basket of breakfast goodies

Stonewall Kitchen New England Breakfast Gift

Check price at Amazon

Breakfast is the most important meal of the day, so treat someone with this basket full of morning meal goodies. This package includes maple syrup, pancake and waffle mix, two types of jam, and a tin of coffee.

The internet's favorite prebiotic soda

Poppi Sparkling Prebiotic Soda Fun Favorites Pack

Check price at Amazon

Who does love a special beverage? Poppi is a trendy "better-for-you" soda brand that is made with unfiltered apple cider vinegar and prebiotics from agave insulin. From personal experience we can confirm that it actually tastes incredible.

A gourmet spice cabinet restock

Check price at Burlap and Barrel

A spice collection is essential for experienced chefs or new cooks. Burlap and Barrel sustainably sources all its spices and ensures maximum freshness in every jar. Having tried its spices ourselves, we can confidently say your giftee will never have had a spice cabinet like this.

Tropical fruits they've never tried before

Miami Fruit Variety Box

Check price at Miami Fruit

Treat your favorite adventurous eater to a box filled with tropical fruits they may have never tried, let alone even heard of, before. Miami Fruit harvests in-season tropical fruits from local farms in South Florida and ships them nationwide. You can't be sure exactly what they'll get in their shipment, and that's part of the fun, but each box usually contains bananas along with other seasonal finds like soursop, sugar apple, guanabana, and more.

A box of tongue-numbing hot sauces

Fuego Box Tame to Insane Hot Sauce Challenge Box

Check price at Amazon

If they watch "Hot Ones" and think I could do that, give them a chance to try with this set of 11 craft hot sauces– also referred to as the Tame To Insane Hot Sauce Challenge. Ranging from mild to hotter-than-hot, and packaged in that order too, this set is ready to test their tongue-tingling tolerance. Once they complete the challenge they can use the sauces to dress up eggs, tacos, chicken wings, or anything they're eating that needs a kick.

Treats for the ultimate sweet tooth

Milk Bar The Sweet Spot

Check price at Milk Bar

It'll be hard to decide what to eat first from this trio of Milk Bar favorites — will it be the moist birthday cake truffles, buttery Milk Bar pie, or the chewy cookies in creative flavors like Blueberry & Cream? 

A flight of wild wine flavors

In Good Taste Wine Flights

Check price at In Good Taste

If they choose wines based on what they're eating and love to try new flavors, In Good Taste's Wine Flight will be the perfect gift as it offers non-traditional wine flavors such as black currant, pineapple, fig, and black cherry. Each flight includes eight tasting bottles and every purchase comes with a complimentary interactive virtual wine tasting.

A carb-heavy box of pastries and bread

Harry & David Signature Bakery Tray

Check price at Harry & David

The tray of cookies, cakes, and other pastries will be a big hit for anyone who loves lingering in bakeries. Reviewers love the lemon poppyseed cake and raspberry galettes. 

A tour of french flavors

Williams Sonoma Tour de France Gift Crate

Check price at Williams Sonoma

Made in collaboration with European culinary experts, this wooden crate is stuffed to the brim with a selection of bites inspired by French haute cuisine like smoked salmon, paté, trout roe, and triple-cream cheese.

A sushi making kit

Uncommon Goods Sushi Making Kit

Check price at Uncommon Goods

With a bamboo rolling mat, sushi rice, rice vinegar powder, nori, sesame seeds, and wasabi powder, all they need to do is add fish to make their favorite sushi rolls at home. The kit even comes with a grocery guide, cooking instructions, and drink pairing suggestions.

A coffee sampler with chocolate pairings

Bean Box Coffee and Chocolate Tasting Box

Check price at Bean Box

Chocolate and coffee are a match made in heaven, and this box set from Bean Box treats the lucky recipient to the best of both. Four bags of artisanal coffee (freshly ground or whole beans) come with four gourmet chocolates and tasting notes. Enjoyed separately or together, there's likely a new favorite somewhere in this box. 

A trio of award-winning cheeses

Williams Sonoma Best of Cellars Jasper Hill Cheese Collection

Check price at Williams Sonoma

Is there any gift better suited for a cheese connoisseur than a trio of artisanal cheeses. This set features three of the most popular picks from the cheesemakers at Jasper Hill, who have won awards for making some of the best dairy delights around: the Little Hosmer, the Willoughby, and the Cabot Clothbound Cheddar.

A variety of unique honeys

Savannah Bee Company Book of Honey

Check price at Savannah Bee Company

Make their life sweeter with this set of six jars of honey. Tupelo, Lavender, Saw Palmetto, Wildflower, Acacia and Orange Blossom honeys each bring a unique flavor profile for them to enjoy.

A complete spread from an iconic deli

Katz's Delicatessen Birthday Box

Check price at Katz's Delicatessen

As the oldest deli in the city, Katz's Delicatessen is a New York City institution. Whether they've been wanting to try a Katz's sandwich ever since watching "When Harry Met Sally" or have been missing the deli's classics since moving far away, this box will be a welcomed gift that probably won't last long. 

Inside they'll find 1 lb. pastrami, 1 lb. corned beef, 1 lb. swiss cheese, a half loaf of rye, 1 quart of half sours, 1 quart of full sours, 8 oz. mustard, 8 oz. reuben dressing, 8 oz. sauerkraut, and 1 package of black and white cookies. It's all the fixings they need to whip up reubens, pastrami sandwiches, and then some.

A bundle of meats straight from Spain

Mercado Famous La Familia Bundle

Check price at Mercado Famous

Help them upgrade their next charcuterie board with a selection of cured meats from Mercado Famous. The brand is bringing the Spanish way of life to the states with its high-quality, organic meats made to savor. This specialty bundle includes one pack each of Jamon 50% Iberico, Chorizo Iberico, Salchichon, and Lomo 50% Iberico.

A box of 32 pieces of mochi ice cream

Mochidoki Mochi Best Sellers Collection

Check price at Mochidoki

Introduce your giftee to the delicious world of mochi, ice cream wrapped in dough made from sticky rice. Mochidoki now ships nationwide, and this pack has 32 pieces in 8 best-selling flavors. The package has enough dry ice to keep everything frozen for up to five hours after delivery. 

A healthy variety of nuts and fruits

Oh! Nuts Holiday Nut & Dried Fruit Gift Basket

Check price at Amazon
Check price at Oh! Nuts

There are a few out-of-the-ordinary fruits in this box: Dried summer plums, papaya, and pear. Pair them with crunchy nuts, meat, and cheese, or enjoy them on their own. 

Unique sweet and savory snacks

CRAVEBOX Gourmet Specialty Snack Box

Check price at Amazon
Check price at CRAVEBOX

CRAVEBOX is chock full of sweet, savory, and healthy snacks like garlic parmesan pretzel sticks or birthday cake graham crackers. They'll be excited to receive snacks that they've likely never heard of before, and it can inspire a reordering of some of their favorites. 

Beautifully designed biscotti

Hazel & Creme Biscotti Gift Baskets

Check price at Amazon

These beautifully decorated, Kosher almond biscotti are an unconventional sweet treat compared to the typical cookie basket. Plus, this box comes pre-wrapped and ready to gift. 

A variety bucket filled with sour flavored candies

Dylan's Candy Bar Nostalgia Gift Bucket

Check price at Dylan’s Candy Bar

This giant bucket is the perfect gift for those who never grew out of their sweet tooth. The retailer also offers other fun options, such as its chocolate lovers' gift basket.

A flavorful pack of seasonings and sauces

Momofuku Pantry Starter Pack

Check price at Momofuku

If they're always looking for ways to add new flavors to their meals, this seasoning starter pack is a great place to start. This set includes unique blends of savory, tingly, and spicy seasoned salts as well as chili crunch, and restaurant-grade tamari and soy sauces. 

A box of Japanese snacks they can't get in the US

Bokksu Gift Snack Box

Check price at Bokksu

Bokksu works directly with snack makers in Japan, so your recipient won't be able to find these unique and delicious treats here. Every month's box has a different theme and contains 20 to 25 snacks, as well as a tea pairing. The first box is called, "Seasons of Japan" and will feature snacks from every season of the year. 

A unique charcuterie assortment you can't find at a regular grocery store

D'artagnan Delicious Duck Cure Bundle

Check price at D'artagnan

Expand their charcuterie horizons with this box of dried and cured duck meats, including duck rillettes, duck prosciutto, smoked duck breast, and duck saucisson sec. There's also a rich black truffle foie gras, which goes well with some bread.

A cheesy collection from a New York City institution

Murray's Cheese Greatest Hits

Check price at Murray’s Cheese

If they crave a mild cheese, there's a manchego. For a sharper edge, try the cheddar. There's also aged gruyere and creamy mini brie. To go along with this sampler of Murray's Cheese's popular varieties, there are cherry preserves, crackers, and almonds in the box as well. 

Eight servings of 'tea drops'

Tea Drops Discovery Tea Sampler

Check price at Tea Drops

Tea Drops are tea leaves that come in fun shapes such as hearts, flowers, and stars. As the name suggests, simply drop the packed tea leaves into boiling water to create a cup of tea. The tea kit contains both caffeinated and non-caffeinated varieties. 

Nostalgia-inducing Mrs. Fields cookies

Mrs. Fields Large Sunshine Basket

Check price at FTD

Your favorite food stand from the mall offers a box of 48 bite-sized cookies, 30 brownie bites, and eight regular cookies that arrives right at their door. It wouldn't be a "Sunshine" box without four frosted sun cookies.
