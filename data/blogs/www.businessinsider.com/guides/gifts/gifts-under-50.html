66 gifts under $50 for those in pursuit of the perfect present

With holiday parties and family gatherings galore, there's plenty of merriment — and stress — on the horizon this time of year. 

To help, we rounded up the best gifts you can find — all for $50 or less. From beloved beauty finds to gourmet bites and cookware, there's something for everyone in your life. We know you're on a mission to find the perfect gift, and so are we, whether its a viral (and surprisingly affordable) personal ice cream maker that will bring pure delight or cozy slipper socks for those in the pursuit of all things hygge.

This gift guide includes a sponsored product that has been suggested by Chewy. It also meets our editorial criteria in terms of quality and value.*

A monogram necklace

Delicate Monogram Necklace, available at Anthropologie

Best for: A personal touch

For fans of minimalist jewelry, this take on the classic monogram necklace features the letter off to the side, giving it a sleek, updated look. 

A trio of spicy sauces and seasonings

Holiday Triple Threat, available at Fly By Jing

Best for: The adventurous eater 

Help them spice up their home cooking with fresh takes on traditional Sichuan flavors. This spicy features the brand's iconic Sichuan Chili Crisp, the umami-rich, tangy Zhong Sauce which is perfect for dumpling-dipping, and the numbingly spicy Mala Spice Mix which can be added to rubs, marinades, batters, and more.

A big box of treats for your pet

Bocce's Bakery Santa Paws Holiday Dog Box, available at Chewy

Best for: The one who loves to spoil their pet

Cats and dogs deserve some love around the holidays, too. Enter gifts from Chewy. Whether you're looking to pamper your own pooch or surprise your friend who's obsessed with their cat, Bocce's Bakery Holiday treat boxes have holiday themed treats for your dog and cats.

*Sponsored by Chewy

A personalized video message from their favorite celebrity

Cameo video, available on Cameo

Best for: The pop culture fanatic

Whether they have a favorite musician, reality TV star, comedian, or actor, there's a good chance their favorite celebrity is on Cameo. You can even choose from pre-recorded video messages with a personal shoutout, or opt to give them a super special one-on-one video call. Of course, the price will greatly vary depending on who the star is.

A Bluetooth karaoke microphone

Bonaok Bluetooth Karaoke Microphone, available at Amazon

Best for: The songbird

This Bluetooth karaoke microphone is the perfect accessory to gift the one who's the life of the party. They can sing along to their favorite songs with this gift that offers premium sound and noise reduction. 

A personal ice cream maker, because why not?

Dash My Mug Ice Cream Maker Machine, available at Amazon, Macy's and Target

Best for: The experimenter 

Dash's My Mug Ice Cream Maker is the little kitchen appliance we never knew we needed but now we're obsessed with. This small but mighty device whips up two cups of your own homemade gelato, sorbet, ice cream, or frozen yogurt within 20 minutes. Get innovative with flavors and mix-ins, and it comes with a recipe guide to get started.

A pair of wireless earbuds

JLab Go Air Pop True Wireless Earbuds, available at Amazon and Walmart

Best for: The one who's always listening to tunes

Our tech reporter describes these $25 earbuds as the "best-kept secret for incredible value in wireless earbuds with better sound quality than their price lets on." Each earbud offers eight hours of audio and is available in lots of fun colors. The charging cable is also conveniently built right into the case, so there is one less cord to keep track of.

The fanny pack they'll forget they're wearing

Patagonia Ultralight Black Hole Mini Hip Pack, available at REI, Dick's Sporting Goods, Patagonia

Best for: The one who's always on the go

Made from 100% recycled ripstop nylon and treated with a silicone face coating for weather resistance, this mini pack is durable and practical. Our pick for the best small fanny pack, the Patagonia Ultralight Blackhole is so light that they'll forget it's on. From the trails to the streets, they'll be secured and ready to go, always.

A spice and herb kit packed with international flavors

Gourmet Oil Dipping Spice Kit, available at Uncommon Goods

Best for: The seasoning aficionado 

Whether it's a dash of Italian oregano or za'atar from the Middle East, any cook will appreciate this international spice kit that lets them cook with various tastes of the world. 

An alternative way to enjoy candles

Candle Warmer Lamp, Available at Amazon

Best for: filling their home with amazing scents

Our executive editor swears by these candle-warming lamps, which make candles last so much longer. The heat from the bulb melts the top layers of wax and fills the room with scent without the need for a flame. The brightness (and heat temperature) and height are adjustable, and you can run a timer.

A unique journal

Embroidered Journal, available at Rifle Paper Co., and Amazon

Best for: The creative journaler

We're inspired to put pen to these stunning pages. This flower-adorned embroidered hardcover journal from Rifle Paper Co. feels bespoke.

A beautifully designed mug

Rika Mug, available at Anthropologie

Best for: The one who loves their beverages 

Anyone who loves their morning coffee knows that the mug said coffee is in is almost as important as the coffee itself. We immediately fell in love with this handpainted pastel mug that comes with a coaster that also converts to a lid to keep drinks hot for longer.

A pair of gold hoops with a twist

Globe Boho Hoops, available at Quince

Best for: The minimalist dresser 

A great pair of gold hoops can make any lackluster outfit feel special and more put-together. If they don't already have a go-to pair, why not give them one? These 18K vermeil gold earrings are the perfect everyday earrings.

A rocks glass etched with a city map

Urban Map Glass, available at Uncommon Goods

Best for: The one who really loves their city (and whiskey)

Whether you want to bring back fond memories of a place you visited together or just want a gift that highlights their favorite stomping grounds, you can cheers to these rocks glasses intricately etched with a city map and interspersed street names. Over 30 major US cities are available to choose from.

To really up the ante, consider pairing the glass with a nice bottle of bourbon. 

A set of spa-worthy shower bombs

Body Restore Bloom Shower Steamers Aromatherapy 6 Pack, available at Amazon

Best for: The person who doesn't have a bathtub but wants the same fun

No bathtub? No problem. With these shower bombs, anyone can transform their bathroom into a makeshift spa, and they don't even need a bathtub to do it. This six-pack, made with essential oils, includes eucalyptus and mint, citrus grove, lavender, jasmine, bergamot spring, and chamomile steamers.

A monogrammed clutch

Palm Leaf Rounded Clutch, available at Mark and Graham

Best for: The beach vacationer

You can get your giftee's initials embroidered on this cute roll-up clutch for a personal touch. The bag is woven from natural palm leaves for a beachy vibe, and the blue and white striped interior adds to the fun coastal feel.

A foam roller for recovery

TriggerPoint Grid Foam Roller, available at Amazon and TPTherapy

Best for: The perpetually sore athlete 

Proper recovery is paramount for optimal performance. That's why this foam roller is such a great gift; it'll help them improve mobility, increase circulation, relieve muscle pain and tightness, and get them in fighting shape all around.

A popular guided journal

BestSelf Self Journal, available at BestSelf and Amazon

Best for: The one who's always talking about their big idea

For anyone with a major goal in mind (even if that goal is just being more organized), this sleek planner will help them get far. It lasts for 13 weeks, making their goal feel more approachable and helping them set a new journaling habit.

A cozy cashmere beanie

Mongolian Cashmere Ribbed Beanie, available at Quince

Best for: The one who loves a warm head

Cold-weather accessories make a great gift for anyone braving the cold this winter and the cashmere ribbed is exactly what they need. Quince specializes in offering cashmere that feels exceptionally luxe with a wallet-friendly pricetag. Made with ethically sourced 100% Grade-A Mongolian cashmere, the unisex design is available in 10 different colors to match a variety of outfits.  

An easy way to make their own bubble tea

Bubble Tea Kit, available at Uncommon Goods

Best for: The boba lover

For the bubble tea drinker in your life, this kit comes with two flavors of loose-leaf tea, tapioca pearls, and two reusable stainless steel straws. All they'll have to do is add a little milk (if they like!) and sip on this delicious brew.

Socks made to be shown off

Polo Ralph Lauren Bear Sock Gift 3-Pack, available at Macy's

Best for: The suave dresser

There's been a renewed fascination with the timeless Polo Ralph Lauren Bears. While the iconic Polo bear sweaters cost nearly $400, you can gift a 3-pack of socks for under $50, with three different designs of the adorable bears.

A meal kit from their favorite restaurant

Restaurant Meal Kits, available at Goldbelly

Best for: The foodie

Bring a bit of their favorite restaurant right to their door. From bagels to barbeque, Goldbelly ships food gifts nationwide from iconic eateries in major cities. 

A personalized pillow of their favorite fur baby

Aurespaces Custom Pet Pillow, available at Etsy

Best for: The proud pet parent

If there's nothing they love more than their cat or dog, this pillow — featuring a blown-up picture of their pet — is sure to make them smile. 

A small but mighty smart assistant

Amazon Echo Dot (5th Generation), available at Amazon

Best for: The multitasker

Give the gift of a virtual helper. The Amazon Echo Dot offers a compact, affordable smart speaker with Alexa built-in. Friends and family members will love being able to dim lights, control the volume on their TV, check the weather, and more, all with just the sound of their voice.

A portable straw that makes water drinkable

LifeStraw Personal Water Filter, available at Amazon

Best for: The outdoor adventurer

This portable, personal water filter is consistently one of the bestselling products on Prime Day. It filters water from creeks and rivers, making it perfect for hiking, camping, and travel.

A sweet and savory collaboration

Green Goddess Olive Oil, available at Kosterina

Best for: The hostess

This limited-edition delicacy, in partnership with Baked by Melissa's Melissa Ben-Ishay, combines 100% pure Extra Virgin Olive Oil with the flavors of Melissa's viral Green Goddess salad featuring zesty basil, garlic, onion, and lemon notes. The perfect dressing for upgrading salads and veggie dishes or simply dunking bread.

A tracker for lost items

Apple AirTag, available at Amazon, Target, and Apple

Best for: Anyone always losing their keys

Whether you lose track of your keys or a bag, Apple's AirTag is a sure way to find any lost item. This water-resistant tracker plays a sound or uses its precise distance findings to lead you straight to the item regardless of the distance on the Find My app.

Cozy house slippers

EQUICK Cloud Slides, available at Amazon

Best for: The lounger

Clean space enthusiasts who enjoy a no outdoor shoe policy in their homes will appreciate these pillow slipper slides. The platform bottom and cushioned sole make for a cute and cozy fit. 

Farm-to-skin lip balms

Beekman 1802 Ten Piece Lip Balm Set, available at Uncommon Goods

Best for: The sustainable beauty fan

This farm-to-skin lip balm set is just right for the green beauty obsessive. All 10 lip balms are made from natural goat's milk and essential oils. Beautifully packaged and scented, the fragrances include Ylang Ylang and Tuberose, Orange Blossom, Fig Leaf, Sweet Grass, Grapefruit, Oak Moss, Apricot and Honey, Vanilla, and an unscented balm.

A cult-favorite candle

Candles, available at Otherland

Best for: The fancy candle collector

We love Otherland's candles, whether seasonally inspired or from the classic collection. Notable scent combinations such as champagne, saffron, and leather, gorgeous packaging, and a 55-hour burn time have deemed Otherland's candles a foolproof gift among the Insider Reviews team.

Read more about the best candle brands.

The cutest selection of hair clips

Jenny Lemons Hair Clips, available at Jenny Lemons

Best for: The playful fashionista

It's hard to choose a favorite when it comes to these delightful food-inspired claw clips. If they have an obsession with pickles, indulge them with a Mini Pickle claw clip. Dreaming of a trip to Paris, or just appreciate carbs, they'll love the quirky Baguette barrettes. Or keep things classic with Jenny Lemons' fruit-themed origins with the Large Lemon and Flowers Hair Claw Clip. And just as charming but less food-themed is this  Midi Discoball that gives Taylor Swift Mirrorball vibes.

Smells like summer

Sol de Janeiro Cheirosa '62 Hair & Body Fragrance Mist, available at Amazon and Sephora

Best for: The one planning their next beach vacation

Many of us are familiar with the social media famous Bum Bum Cream, but fans of Sol de Janeiro shouldn't sleep on the Hair and Body Fragrance Mist. The Cherirosa '62 smells absolutely divine, like like sunshine and the beach, or more officially, salted caramel, pistachio, and vanilla. Spray it everywhere liberally. The 3-ounce size is also travel-friendly.

Authentic Japanese ramen

ICHIRAN Take-Home Ramen Kit, available at Amazon and ICHIRAN USA

Best for: The ramen lover

ICHIRAN Ramen has been delighting ramen aficionados since 1960. Known for its original Tonkatsu (pork bone) soup, its ramen combines the rich umami flavors of the broth with Hakata-style noodles that are slender, chewy, and perfect. Enjoy a little bit of heat? The kit also comes with ICHIRAN's original spicy seasoning, using togarashi pepper spice to amp up the flavors even more.

The water bottle of the moment

Owala FreeSip Water Bottle, Available at Amazon, Target, and Walmart

Best for: Keeping them up with the trends

The latest collectable water bottle of the moment, is the Owala FreeSip. Beyond the fun color combinations, the Owala keeps drinks cold for 24 hours, is spill-proof, has two ways to drink from, and is easy to keep clean.

A streaming stick that turns any TV into a smart one

Roku Streaming Stick 4K, available at Amazon, Walmart, and Roku

Best for: The TV fanatic

Upgrade their Netflix marathons without actually buying them a whole new TV. The Roku Streaming Stick 4K offers 4K, HD, and HDR streaming in a portable package and at an affordable price. 

A set of loose-leaf teas that Oprah loves

Vadham Chai Tea Reserve Set, available at Amazon

Best for: The tea drinker who wants to spice it up

This classy set of loose-leaf teas made it into Oprah's Favorite Things back in 2018. It's filled with three variations of chai tea that any tea lover will appreciate. 

A puzzle that transforms into art

Let There Be Cake, Katie Smith 800-piece puzzle, available at Jiggy

Best for: The hobbiest who loves to show off their activities

Jiggy's artist-driven puzzles turn a timeless, relaxing activity into a work of art. They even include puzzle glue once you finish your masterpiece to seal it. Jiggy spotlights various female artists, all of whom receive a percentage of every puzzle sold. In addition to their fun, modern designs, Jiggy also offers a create-your-own personalized jigsaw puzzle for a truly sentimental gift.

Delicious mini cupcakes

Cupcake Gift Boxes, available at Baked by Melissa

Best for: Anyone with a sweet tooth

Who doesn't love getting a sweet surprise? With delicious flavors ranging from Cookie Dough to Pink Frosted Donut, these bite-size treats are sure to please. You can also add a special gift box to complete the gift. 

A set of magnets that are fun to play with and can boost concentration

Speks 2.5mm Magnet Balls, available at Speks and Barnes & Noble

Best for: The fidgeter 

Almost everyone on the Insider Reviews team has a set of Speks at their desk. The little magnetic balls can be mashed, molded, and built into fun shapes and are a fun fidget toy that even adults will love. 

Socks meet slippers

Bombas Gripper Slipper, available at Bombas for women and men

Best for: The homebody

Bomba's grippy sock-like slipper combines everything that makes Bombas socks amazing with the comfort and coziness of slippers. Available in both men's and women's sizing, these indoor shoes have a grippy bottom to prevent sliding and provide the familiar compression of a sock. They're machine-washable too.

A taste of Japan by way of snacks

3-Month Snack Gift Box, available at Bokksu

Best for: Anyone bored of their current snack rotation

Adventurous foodies will love the chance to taste-test a curated box of gourmet Japanese snacks. In this Bokksu box, they can expect to find between 10-14 snacks, a tea pairing, and an in-depth guide that details every product included.

A hat she'll wear as a badge of honor

Favorite Daughter Classic Logo Cotton Twill Baseball Cap, available at Nordstrom, Revolve, and Favorite Daughter

Best for: Your favorite daughter

Ideally, for the girl in your life who is an only child or daughter, let her know she's your favorite with this statement-making baseball cap from the label of the same name.

A funky-colored kettle

Bodum Electric Kettle, available at MoMA Design Store

Best for: Lover of classics with a twist

Sure, Bodum is known for its excellent tea and coffee accessories, but this exclusive collaboration as a spotlight on good design with the MoMA Design store surely caught our eye. This color-blocked electric kettle decked out in primary colors is one they'll surely keep out on display and within arms reach for daily warm beverages. If you're feeling generous, you can also gift the matching mugs, coordinating French Press, mugs, or tea pot.

A just-launched cookbook

Half Baked Harvest Quick & Cozy: A Cookbook by Tieghan Gerard, available at Amazon, Bookshop, and Barnes and Noble

Best for: The aspiring chef

With a focus on quick and easy meals, Tieghan Gerard of Half Baked Harvest is back with her fourth cookbook. It's filled with 120 recipes. Most of them can be ready in under 45 minutes and in just one pan. We're eying Garlic Butter Steak Bites with Bang Bang Sauce and Double-Baked Tacos, both of which are full of flavor and so attainable to make.

Cozy knit gloves

Colorblock Knit Touchscreen Gloves, available at Verloop

Best for: The one who is always cold

Verloop crafts its colorful knit accessories out of excess deadstock yarn. Gift these colorblock gloves to the person who won't feel shy from a burst of color. The'll keep hands nice and toasty, with a high cuff to keep cold air out. They also have Nano-metallic knit fingertips so wearers can continue to use touchscreen devices.

A bestselling face mask for clearer skin

Aztec Secret Indian Healing Mask, available at Amazon, Target and Walmart

Best for: The one in search of the best skincare products

Anyone in on the latest skincare trends will know about this mask. Many claim it has helped clear their skin, and it has over 74,000 five-star reviews on Amazon. The best part is that this powerful facial is just $15.

A luxurious lotion to keep skin hydrated

Necessaire The Body Lotion, available at Sephora and Amazon

Best for: The one dreading winter skin

Necessaire's clean beauty products come in beautiful, minimalist packaging that looks as good in their bathroom as it feels on their skin. This cult-favorite will help them moisturize dry winter skin for good.

A simple and elegant photo calendar

Walnut Desktop Photo Calendar, available at Artifact Uprising

Best for: The fan of the old-school paper calendar

All of Artifact Uprising's customized photo gifts are simple, beautiful, and made from eco-friendly materials. This simple calendar is an easy choice for anyone on your list. Just pick 12 photos (one for each month) of the people and places they love most to add a special sentiment to their desk setup.

Level up their bakeware

Staub 2-Piece Rectangular Ceramic Baking Set, available at Wayfair and Crate and Barrel

Best for: The baker

Staub's ceramic baking set effortlessly goes from oven to table and offers beautiful, even heating. the From brownies to pasta bakes, these ceramic vessels will become a go-to.

A sheet mask that'll hydrate dry, stressed skin

Dr. Jart+ Soothing Hydra Solution Pro, available at Sephora and Amazon

Best for: The face mask fan

Fall and winter skin tends to be dry and dull. While you can't change the weather, you can throw on a hydrating face mask to stay moisturized. This one will add lots of soothing hydration to their skin to keep it feeling fresh.

A cold brew coffee maker to keep up with their iced coffee habit

Takeya Cold Brew Coffee Maker, available at Amazon and Target

Best for: The at-home coffee brewer

If their morning ritual includes a cup of cold brew, they'll appreciate this convenient cold brew maker. All they have to do is fill it with their favorite coffee grinds, add water, let it sit, and they've got a glass of delicious cold brew on the way. 

Makeup towels that make washing their face less of a chore

Makeup Towels, available at Weezie

Best for: The one who struggles to remove their mascara

If they've never thought of washcloths as anything special, Weezie towels will change their minds. The adorable towels are embroidered with winky eyelids, the word "make up," or can be customized with a monogram, phrase, or icon of your choice. Plus, the dark navy blue color will conceal makeup stains.

A spray that protects their shoes

Jason Markk Repel, available at Amazon and Nordstrom

Best for: The sneakerhead

If they love shoes, they should have the right products to take care of their favorite footwear. This spray protects shoes from stains caused by water and oil, and there are multiple people on our team who swear by it for every new pair of shoes they get.

Jewelry for their handbags

Lenora Dame Heart of Gold Chain Bag Charm, available at Etsy

Best for: Anyone with a handbag obsession

Bag charms have become the must-have of the season. Instead of adding another bag to their collection, let them personalize their bag with a bag charm. While there are tons of high-end designer options out there, you don't have to spend a ton. Lenora Dame on Etsy has leaned into the trend with unexpected beaded and charm-filled chains for bags. They're whimsical and fun.

A lip gloss that has all the benefits of a balm

Tower28 Beauty Jelly Lip Gloss, available at Sephora and Tower28

Best for: Anyone complaining about dry lips

This clean-beauty favorite delivers the glossy sheen they'd expect from a lip gloss, but it's loaded with nourishing oils to keep lips hydrated, too. 

Fancy markers

Ohuhu 30 Colors Acrylic Paint Pens, available at Amazon

Best for: The artist

Encourage them to pick up a new hobby (or take advantage of an existing one) with this dual-tip paint pen set that includes 30 highly pigmented markers in a storage case to keep everything organized.

Pair with a sketchbook for the ultimate set, but they can also be used on a variety of surfaces like ceramic, mugs, glasses, plastic, canvas, rocks, wood or fabric.

A fitting vehicle for their post-run brews

Etched Marathon Pint Glass, available at Uncommon Goods

Best for: The runner 

If they like celebrating a long run with a big pint, they'll appreciate these pint glasses etched with famous marathon routes. 

A bold lip color that'll last all day long

Lancome L'Absolu Rouge Lipstick, available at Sephora and Macy's

Best for: The one who sticks to one lipstick at a time

Whether it's a pale peach or a deep-red look they crave, they'll love this long-lasting lipstick with its saturated colors and hydrating formula.

An indoor putting green for the rec-room or office

Putt-A-Bout Par Three Putting Green, available at Amazon

Best for: The golfer

Whether just getting into the game or they're a seasoned pro, an at-home putting game lets them practice their technique, and maybe blow off a little steam throughout the day. A book subscription

3-Month Subscription, available at Book of the Month

A subscription box that sends them an exciting new read catered to their tastes each month is the perfect gift for a bookworm.

Hydro Flask's Travel Coffee Mug is a team favorite. It combines the classic shape of a mug with Hydro Flask's TempShield insulation to keep beverages hot, or cold, for hours— a great gift for the coffee or tea lover who's always on the move. 

A cute reusable tote that can fit tons of stuff

Standard Baggu, available at Baggu

It's no wonder these bags are bestsellers — they can hold up to 50 pounds of stuff and come in a range of fun colors and patterns. Plus, at just $14, they're a great deal.

Leather Charger Roll-Up, available at Mark & Graham

Best for: The expert packer

After they fill the three pockets with cables and chargers, all they have do is roll everything up and they're good to go. The soft, supple leather comes in a variety of fun colors and patterns. 

A cult-favorite cast-iron skillet

Lodge Seasoned Cast Iron Skillet, available at Williams Sonoma, Target, and Amazon

Best for: The frequent home cook

Every cook needs a cast-iron skillet in their kitchen. Lodge makes some of the best out there, but at prices that won't break the bank. 

A candle that reminds them of their favorite place

Homesick Location Scented Candle, available at Amazon, Homesick, and Uncommon Goods

Best for: The one with state pride 

This is a great gift that's sure to make anyone sentimental. Whether it's their hometown, college town, or favorite spot to vacation, a Homesick candle, with scents inspired by all sorts of locations, will bring them back to that favorite place. 

An apron loaded with plenty of clever features

Five Two Ultimate Apron, available at Food52

Best for: The kitchen splatter-averse

Anyone who spends a good amount of time in the kitchen will appreciate this durable apron with its sturdy fabric, clever pockets made to hold the essentials, and pot holders built right in.
