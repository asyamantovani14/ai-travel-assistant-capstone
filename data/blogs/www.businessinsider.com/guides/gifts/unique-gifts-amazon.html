60 quirky and useful gifts from Amazon that ship quick, from a candle warmer to 3D puzzles

If you need a gift fast, there's no better place to shop than Amazon for gifts, thanks to quick Prime two-day shipping. The beauty of the site, especially if for a last minute gift is that you can find items that are useful or unique, and sometimes both.

From funky home decor to quirky apparel, there are plenty of unique gifts you can buy on Amazon. Tap into their sense of humor with a silly gag gift, like a bread-shaped pillow, or go for a practical product like a candle warmer, so they can spread fragrance without burning through their favorite candles.

Whatever way you go, you can't go wrong with any of these gifts. Keep scrolling for 60 unique gifts you can find on Amazon for everyone on your list, from the future sister-in-law you're hoping to impress to the coworker who's had your back all year long.

A baguette-shaped pillow

3D Simulation Bread Plush Pillow at Amazon

For any and all bread lovers, this realistic-looking novelty pillow is a funny and supremely cozy addition to any sleep setup.

A calming bonsai growing kit

Planter's Choice Bonsai Starter Kit at Amazon

An ideal gift for those with a nurturing spirit, the Planter's Choice Bonsai Starter Kit is a great introduction to gardening and is a calming addition to their desk, nightstand, or entryway table. The kit comes with a learning guide, seeds stored in vials for germination, four biodegradable growing pots, one expanding-soil disc, four bamboo plant markers, and one bonsai clipper.

A book filled with random interesting facts

"The Book of Unusual Knowledge" at Amazon

This book is huge, with 704 pages of information they never knew they needed to know. From politics to sports, the contents truly run the gamut, making it a fun read for anyone who loves to learn.

A 3D puzzle that doubles as decor

Pintoo 3D Puzzle Vase at Amazon
Take their puzzle hobby to the next level with this 3D vase, designed to be displayed once completed. It comprises 160 interlocking plastic pieces and includes a stand and water cup, so it's fully functional for use with fresh flowers. We can't get enough of the designs that resemble fine china.

A retro-looking rice cooker

Dash Mini Rice Cooker at Amazon

This little gadget not only cooks rice but can make stews, oatmeal, soup, pasta, veggies, and more. The style and colorful enamel coating add a retro look to their kitchen.

Slippers that collect dust as you walk

Selric Bear Microfiber Mop Slippers at Amazon

No matter how much you clean your home, your floors get dusty. These slippers are a cute solution to that problem. Not only are they a cozy place for their feet, but they actually pick up dust thanks to the microfiber bottoms that function like a mop.

A fun desk accessory makes for the perfect white elephant gift

Wacky Waving Inflatable Tube Guy at Amazon

This 17-inch waving tube man is simply silly, and even includes a 32-page mini book exploring the origins of the iconic flailing arm man.

A candle that smells like their favorite place

Homesick Candle at Amazon

There's no place like home. Homesick Candles taps into that widely held sentiment with its line of location-inspired scents. Treat them to a candle that whisks them back home with every waft.

Their new favorite beverage accessory

The Draft Top Lift Universal — Topless Can Opener at Amazon

Have you ever needed to take your can's top off? Well, as seen on Shark Tank, The Draft Top Lift smoothly turns your favorite sodas, seltzers, and beer into open beverages, providing an opportunity to sip, garnish, or take in the aroma of your drink of choice. The nifty tool works on mini, skinny, standard, and tall size cans. There is definitely a learning curve, but this may become your giftee's new favorite party trick at the tailgate.

A comfy fleece blanket they can wear all winter

Pavilia Premium Fleece Blanket with Sleeves for Adults at Amazon

Available in a wide variety of styles and prints (from plaid to burrito), this fleece blanket comes with sleeves and a front pocket to provide maximum, wearable comfort all fall and winter long.

A wireless Bluetooth karaoke microphone

Wireless Bluetooth Karaoke Microphone at Amazon

A must-have for anyone who regularly hosts karaoke gatherings, this Bluetooth microphone lets you connect your phone to play music through its speakers, as well as turn the volume up and down depending on your comfort. It's the perfect thing to shake up a night in or to break the ice at a gathering.

A bamboo bath tray for luxurious soaks

Luxury Bathtub Caddy Tray at Amazon

A bamboo bath tray is one of the best gifts one of our editors ever received. This model comes with extendable sides to fit their tub, silicone grips to keep it in place, and a range of compartments that store everything from books and tablets to wine glasses and soap.

A case that makes their phone look retro

UnnFiko 3D Silicone Phone Case at Amazon

Tap into their nostalgic side with this retro phone case. Not only does the design make for a funky accessory, but it will definitely keep their phone safe from cracks and scratches.

A set of mini mugs for espresso shots

Foster & Rye Camping Mug Shot Glasses (Set of 2) at Amazon

Espresso, ginger shots, and even alcohol tastes better when it's taken out of this cute mini shot glasses. These ones are inspired by camping mugs for a rugged twist. 

An easy attachment to make homemade pasta

KitchenAid Gourmet Pasta Press Attachment at Amazon

If your giftee already has a Kitchenaid stand mixer, get them this attachment that lets them easily make their own fresh noodles. Six different interchangeable discs allow them to switch between noodle types, including spaghetti, bucatini, rigatoni, fusilli, and large or small macaroni.

A candle warmer lamp that makes your favorite candles last longer

Candle Warmer Lamp at Amazon

Our team has slowly, yet surely become candle-warmer converts, which use the heat emitted from the lamp to melt and diffuse the wax from your favorite candles. It's far safer than lighting a candle, and it has a shut-off timer and various heat intensity settings.

A punny dish towel

Moonlight Makers Store Kitchen Towel at Amazon

For the home chef with a penchant for puns, go for this dish towel laden with a cheesy joke.

A massive tote bag with wheels

The Hulken Reusable Grocery Bag on Wheels at Amazon

The untold secret of city dwellers on the go, The Hulken is made for schlepping everything and anything you could want to take in a day — and it's much more attractive than the typical granny carts. It effortlessly rolls alongside you but is also light to carry around and folds flat when not in use. This massive carry-all tote carries the equivalent of six grocery bags. It's bound to become a go-to for groceries, laundry, travel, errands, and more.

A travel pillow that actually works

Trtl Travel Pillow at Amazon

We've sung the praises of the Trtl Pillow before — it's essential for any traveler. The cozy and supportive neck pillow is a great gift for any frequent traveler or anyone who's about to head out on a trip. 

A bestselling cheese board with all the fixings

Smirly Bamboo Cheese Board and Knife Set: Large Charcuterie Boards Set & Cheese Platter at Amazon

For the person who lives to host (or just really loves cheese), this cheese board comes with a drawer for extra storage, a matching fruit tray, and a set of cheese knives to make their wildest charcuterie dreams come true.

Lightsaber chopsticks for 'Star Wars' fans

Chop Sabers Light Up Lightsaber Chopsticks at Amazon

May the force be with them at every meal with these lightsaber chopsticks. A fun addition to any dining table, these LED chopsticks come in various neon colors to suit their mood.

An original Tamagotchi

Original Tamagotchi at Amazon

For those nostalgic for the '90s, there's nothing like playing an OG Tamagotchi. They'll love getting to feed and play with their digital pet.

A heated eye mask with music

Renpho Eye Massager at Amazon

Help them unwind from a long day and mentally turn on their "do not disturb mood" with the Renpho eye massager. The built-in heating pads relieve eye strain and stimulate blood circulation via acupuncture points. It even features a Bluetooth music option so they can put on soothing music to further relax.

An insulated pint glass that keeps brews cool

BruMate HOPSULATOR at Amazon

If they like their beverages ice-cold, they'll appreciate this thoughtful bottle holder. It can hold 12- and 16-ounce cans, or be used as a pint glass holding 16 fluid ounces, so it's really a three-in-one gadget. When they need a break from beer, this is perfect for keeping their water bottles chilled too. 

A mini fire pit for tableside s’mores

Roundfire Tabletop Fire Pit with Smores Kit at Amazon

There seems to be no slowdown in the popularity of fire pits, and this mini tabletop design is perfect for making s'mores -- it even comes with roasting sticks. The Roundfire concrete fire pit works with bioethanol fuels and stays ignited for up to an hour.

A tool to relieve foot pain

TheraFlow Foot Roller at Amazon

This small tool can relieve foot pain, stimulate blood circulation, and help de-stress after long days on their feet.

A fun inflatable lounger

Wekapo Inflatable Lounger Air Sofa at Amazon

From a backyard hangout to a beach picnic, this inflatable lounger will come in handy as a comfy seating option. No pump is required, it comes in a handy carrying case, and it's available in 16 different colors and patterns.

Emulate a Taylor Swift-approved beauty look

Fazit Makeup Patches at Amazon

Whether you're looking for a gift for the die-hard Swiftie in your life or have a friend who can't resist getting glam for dinner or festivals, these Fazit freckled face glitter patches are an unexpected fun way to add sparkle to a makeup look. They're sold in packs of six.

A charging stand for all of their devices

JoyGeek 3 in 1 Wireless Charging Station at Amazon

This gift is a no-brainer for loyal Apple users. Instead of dealing with a tangle of cords from charging their products, they can streamline with this singular hub, which has a designated spot for their iPhone, Airpods, and Apple Watch. 

Soft butter whenever they need it

Norpro Glazed Stoneware Butter Keeper at Amazon

Butter bells, made popular by TikTok, keep your butter soft and fresh for up to 30 days. Just fill the bell with your butter, submerge in clean water, and enjoy. It means your butter stays clean and spreadable without hardening in the fridge.

A vase your favorite bookworm will appreciate

Acrylic Book Vase at Amazon

This acrylic vase, in the shape of a book, highlights the classics and will look gorgeous on any table or shelf.

A suction-cup mount that sticks to their phone case and any smooth surface

Octobuddy Silicone Suction Phone Case Adhesive Mount at Amazon

For taking selfies and recording videos or streaming videos hands-free, this Octobuddy has become a must-have for any content creator or social media obsessed. It easily and endlessly suctions to any smooth surface like glass or a mirror.

A carry-on that turns into a personal item

With the rise of paying to bring a carry-on on many airlines, game the system with Take Off's rolling carry on that converts into a personal item by removing the wheels. Your frequent flyer loved ones will love you for all the fees you've helped them avoid.

A retro-looking speaker with modern conveniences

A nod to all things vintage, this Victrola Bluetooth Radio and speaker can play their favorite tunes while looking like a retro blast from the past.

A soothing desk fountain

A nifty spoon rest for a crabby chef

This adorable cooking companion, Red the Crab, is both a spoon rest and a steam release. It's BPA-free and made of heat-resistant 100% food grade silicone.

A portable printer that lets them print instantly

Nowadays, most of our photos live on our smartphones and social media apps, but many of us still love looking through or hanging up printed pictures. This portable printer lets them do that super easily and with no wires, so they can print pictures in a snap.

A trio of flavorful honey

Bushwick Kitchen Bees Knees Honey Sampler Gift Set at Amazon

If they can't bear the thought of a meal without something sweet, upgrade their condiments with this set of funky-flavored honey from Bushwick Kitchen. 

Premium Silly Putty

Crazy Aaron's Liquid Glass Thinking Putty at Amazon

Fun for kids and adults alike, Crazy Aaron's Thinking Putty brings back the nostalgia of Silly Putty with a ton of different colors, textures, and scents. From Liquid Glass to Glow Putty, there's a fun option for every user.

A warmer to keep their coffee hot

Dimux Coffee Mug Warmer at Amazon

The Dimux coffee mug warmer is a necessary addition to any tea or coffee lover's workstation and keeps beverages at a constant temperature of 131 degrees Fahrenheit. It can also work with their favorite candle to gently release the scent without the need to actually light it.

Bring nature to their window

Window Bird Feeder at Amazon

For aspiring bird watchers or bird enthusiasts, see what species you can attract to their window. This small acrylic bird feeder attaches to an exterior window with adhesive and features a covered area to store food and a separate bird bath. Perhaps help them get started with some best bird seed too.

Dessert plates with a side of dopamine

Ban.do Melamine Dessert Plates at Amazon

These colorful plates are ideal for the entertainer, and a happy addition to any meal to plate appetizers, tapas, or dessert. Plus, they come in a giftable, clear box.

The perfect socks for pizza lovers

Pizza Socks Box (4 pairs) at Amazon

From pizza ovens to apparel, there's no shortage of gifts for pizza lovers. We're partial to these socks because their packaging looks like an actual pizza in an actual pizza box. 

A cooling cap that relieves everything from headaches to hangovers

TheraICE Migraine Relief Cap at Amazon

Nursing a headache or a hangover is the absolute worst. Save the day by introducing them to this cold compress that's gone viral for its ability to soothe aches and comfortably mold to the head. Just place in the freezer two hours for two hours before use. It works hot too.

Packing cubes that get them pumped for their trips

Compression packing cube set for carryon luggage at Amazon

Packing cubes are one of those gifts that your recipient will use all the time (and endlessly thank you for), but they can feel a little bland as a gift. This set, featuring pretty prints of different landscapes, will get them even more excited for their upcoming national park trip (especially when packing all their outfits just got so much easier).

A pack of sticky notes to keep them on track

For the friend who likes to keep it really organized, help them stay on track with this thoughtful set of sticky notes. With categories like "To Ponder," "To Remember," and "To Buy," they can keep track of everything they need to accomplish in the near future. 

A cute and practical phone holder

Dinosaur Silicone Phone Holder at Amazon

This endearing little dino isn't just a piece of decor; it can actually hold a phone.

Beautiful bookends that double as decor

These gorgeous geode bookends add a magical touch to home decor and come in a range of colors from teal to amethyst. You can also choose from various weights and they come with rubber bumpers to ensure bookcases and shelves don't get scratched.

A keychain for hot sauce lovers

Tabasco Hot Sauce Keychain at Amazon

They'll never have to endure a flavorless meal again. This mini bottle of Tabasco sauce comes with a keychain for spice that goes wherever they do. 

Decorative tealights to add some greenery

If even simple succulents seem like too much work to take care of, these cute candle tealights are handmade in the shape of cacti and succulents. They offer a fun touch of green decor for any space.

A fruity hand cream that’ll look great on their counter

This rich hand cream is made with shea butter and peach extract to moisturize seriously dry skin. We love the refreshing, light peach scent and the adorable container, which looks just like a peach. 

A LEGO-style coffee mug

Simple illustrated prints to hang around the house

Finding unique home decor that's also affordable can be a tough feat, but these artistic prints check off all the boxes. The illustrations of plants make a great addition to their kitchen, or even their bedroom if they want to show off their green thumb.
