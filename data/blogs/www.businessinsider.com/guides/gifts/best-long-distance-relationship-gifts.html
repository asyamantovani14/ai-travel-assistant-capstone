42 long-distance relationship gifts to keep each other close

Distance may make the heart grow fonder, but it sure can make the days feel long and lonely. Whether you're looking for the perfect anniversary gift or want to send a treat "just because," we've rounded up a range of ideas that will make the distance seem not so far. Read on for our picks for the best long-distance relationship gifts.

A bracelet that vibrates when your partner is thinking of you

Long Distance Touch Bracelet Set

Check price at Uncommon Goods

This bracelet set helps show your love from afar: Simply touch your bracelet and the other half on your partner's wrist will light up and vibrate to show you're thinking of them.

A jar of pre-written love messages

Message Pill Co. Long Distance Messages in a Bottle

Check price at Amazon

"I miss you" text messages are too predictable. Instead, send these pre-written love messages tucked away in capsules for your partner to open once a day.

An art piece with a custom Spotify playlist

CustomAcrylicUS Song Plague

Check price at Etsy

This acrylic art lets your partner scan a custom song code to pull up a playlist you've created of all the tunes that remind you of each other. The art piece comes on a wooden stand, so it doubles as something nice to look at on the nightstand.

An instant love letter

Lovebox Spinning Heart Messenger

Check price at Uncommon Goods

Love letters in the mailbox are great, but this unique gift lets you deliver them instantly. After sending them the physical box, you can send your partner special messages via the app and a large red heart spins on their box to inform them their digital love letter inside is ready to read.

A set of aesthetically-pleasing touch lamps

LuvLink Set of Two Friendship Lamps

Check price at Amazon
Check price at Etsy

Even though you miss them constantly, sometimes a text message here and there doesn't feel like it gets your emotions across. When you touch your lamp, theirs lights up no matter where or how far away they are — a soothing and subtle reminder of your affection that can be placed on their nightstand or desk.

A video message from their favorite celebrity

Cameo Message

Check price at Cameo

Whether you love watching Bravo shows together or they'd be tickled to hear a "happy birthday" from a "Sopranos" actor, Cameo lets you pay talent to record special messages. Prices can really range depending on who you pick, so this can also be a great gift to split amongst friends or family.

A custom care package filled with all of their favorite things

Happy Box Custom Care Package

Check price at Happy Box

Build a box filled with special treats — from cozy candles to unique coffee to self-care items — to light up your partner's day. Whether they need a pick-me-up on an ordinary day or a celebratory gift, this custom box will make them feel loved from afar.

A game designed to keep each other close

We're Not Really Strangers Card Game

Check price at Amazon
Check price at Walmart
Check price at Target

This card game, from the popular Instagram account We're Not Really Strangers, is designed to enhance connections through a series of questions. It's a thoughtful activity you can enjoy with your partner over the phone or video chat.

A custom search-and-find book with a romantic storyline

Your Super Epic Love Quest Book

Check price at Wonderbly

Create your very own "Where's Waldo" picture search book with custom characters for you and your partner. The storyline is about two lovers finding each other through time and space; an apt theme for any long-distance couple.

Matching bracelets of your names in Morse code

WishANDdesign Couple Name Morse Code Bracelets

Check price at Etsy

If you prefer more subtle displays of your love, you can get these simple bracelets of each other's names in Morse code. You can pick your cord colors and beads for extra customization as well.

Fresh flowers delivered to their door

Urban Stems Flower Delivery

Check price at UrbanStems

Nothing brightens up a lonely room or says, "Wish I could be there" for special occasions you're missing quite like a gorgeous bouquet of flowers. Urban Stems is the best flower delivery service we've tested with so many different arrangements, perfect for any partner.

A pair of matching undies

MeUndies Matching Pairs

Check price at MeUndies

A pair (or two) of fun, pj-worthy MeUndies makes it so easy to match your bottom half to your better half. Choose from countless colors and designs, including seasonal prints and fandom-inspired wear.

Check price at Amazon
Check price at Nordstrom
Check price at Aura Frames

A digital picture frame will brighten up their home with a selection of your favorite photos together. With unlimited storage, you'll be able to consistently update the selection by sending photos and videos directly to their frame using Aura's app.

A virtual portrait painting class

Check price at Uncommon Goods

Being apart doesn't mean you can't still have date nights. This 90-minute virtual class will walk you and your partner through the process of painting each other's portraits. You'll learn something and take home a memento… or at the very least have a good laugh at each other's artistic "skills."

A controller in their favorite color to play online games together

8BitDo Ultimate 2C Wireless Controller

Check price at Amazon

Playing video games together is a great way to stay connected from anywhere. This inexpensive controller comes in a variety of colors to suit their preferences. A box of loving to-do dates

With this custom love ticket box, you can still have special dates with one another despite the distance.

A sweet reminder every morning

Check price at Uncommon Goods

Check price at Amazon
Check price at Barnes & Noble
Check price at Homesick

A custom puzzle of a treasured moment

Check price at JIGGY

Commemorate a favorite memory together with a personalized puzzle until you can make more memories in person. Trouble picking the perfect photo? JIGGY has plenty of beautiful designs made by female artists to choose from, each of which come with glue to keep the puzzle as wall art once completed.

A modern day mixtape

Check price at Etsy

Remember when "I made you a mixtape" was the most romantic thing in the world? This retro-style USB drive will take them back to the good ol' days. Even with a more modern-day, practical twist, you can load this bad boy up with all the songs you need to prove your devotion.

A custom light box with a sweet message

MagicWoOod LDR Gift Custom Magic Box

Check price at Etsy

Check price at Amazon
Check price at Nordstrom
Check price at Bearaby

Check price at Amazon
Check price at Courant

A pottery kit to do together

Check price at Sculpd

Check price at Etsy

A photo book of your favorite memories

Check price at Chatbooks

Custom map artwork
