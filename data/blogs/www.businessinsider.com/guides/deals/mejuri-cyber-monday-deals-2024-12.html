FAQs about Mejuri's Cyber Monday sale

What is Mejuri's rewards program?

Mejuri's rewards program, M+, is completely free to join. Customers can easily do so in-store, online, or via the Mejuri app. Additional perks include free shipping on Monday, exclusive birthday styling sessions and offers, and priority invites to in-store occasions and experiences. M+ Members also earn early access to Mejuri sale events.

When does the Mejuri Cyber Monday sale end?

Mejuri's Cyber Monday sale wraps up today, December 2, 2024.

Is Cyber Monday a good time to shop Mejuri jewelry?

Yes — this marks Mejuri's biggest sale of the year, making it the optimal time to snag new fine jewelry pieces (diamonds included!). You can earn free shipping when you spend over $75, and every piece comes with a 2-year warranty.

Are there any exclusions?

The only exclusions are gift cards and services, such as piercing fees. That said, it is worth noting that you must spend a minimum of $150 to take advantage of the 25% off.
