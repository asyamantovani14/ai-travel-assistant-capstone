4 things to skip from the Nordstrom Anniversary Sale 

Nordstrom-Made brand clothing

For a long time, most of my closet was comprised of Halogen, Caslon, Open Edit, Treasure & Bond, and Chelsea28; in fact, much of it still is. I do love the quality, value, and designs from these brands. However, most of these items will still be on sale come the next Nordstrom sale in October, and the discounts may even be deeper. If there is a piece you love and feels special, go for it. Mine was a geometric print Halogen satin skirt that was sold out in my size by 12:02 a.m. when I was able to shop my cardholder tier. But don't be surprised to see trendier pieces and even some basics back on the rack during the next sale cycle. Of course, there are exceptions to everything, like a great leather jacket or this pajama set. 

Winter coats

It's the dead of summer; unless you live somewhere cold and need a coat right now, you're not going to be missing out on savings on cold weather staples from Bernando, Avec Les Filles, or Sam Edelman in a few week's time. 

Handbags

Sadly, I always find the handbag offerings to be basic and lackluster. It's the same rotation year after year from Coach, Kate Spade, Rebecca Minkoff, AllSaints, and Kurt Geiger, many of which can be found at Nordstrom Rack almost any time of the year. My exceptions to this are luggage (Tumi, Beis, and Monos) and the Longchamp Le Pliage Nylon Tote Bag.  

Designer sunglasses

The Nordstrom Anniversary Sale is full of Gucci, Saint Laurent, Tom Ford, and Ray Bans sunnies galore. While very tempting for those after luxury, give it a few months, and you can likely find the same or similar shades at Nordstrom Rack for around $150.

What are the discounts like?

The Nordstrom Anniversary Sale uniquely positions itself as an opportunity to shop the upcoming season's trends at a temporary discount (usually about 33% off), plus cop savings on items that almost never go on sale and snag exclusives on items released just for the event. 

When is the Nordstrom Anniversary Sale? 

The 2024 Nordstrom Anniversary Sale is open to everyone from July 14 until August 4. Nordstrom credit card holders were able to shop as early as July 9.
