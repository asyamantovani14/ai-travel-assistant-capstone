    Amazon's 2025 Summer Beauty Event is officially live, along with some of the retailer's best deals on makeup, skincare, haircare, fragrances, and personal care we've seen all year. Among the long list of markdowns are plenty of editor-favorite brands like Laneige, CeraVe, Gucci, and more. Whether you need to stock up on a few products or refresh your entire skincare routine, now's the time to shop.

    Each day of the Summer Beauty Event, Amazon is running special deals on certain product categories. On one day, you'll find the best makeup deals, while skincare or grooming might take the lead on the next. With deals rotating daily, planning out your shopping strategy is key. Here's a look ahead to what you can expect during the first week of the sale.

    Amazon Summer Beauty Event daily deals

    • Monday, April 28: Save up to 40% on makeup
    • Tuesday, April 29: Save up to 30% on skincare
    • Wednesday, April 30: Save up to 30% on haircare and tools
    • Thursday, May 1: Save up to 40% on fragrances
    • Friday, May 2: Save up to 35% on men's grooming
    • Saturday, May 3: Save up to 40% on health and wellness
    • Sunday, May 4: Save up to 35% on personal care

    With so many editor-favorite brands and products on sale, you might be too overwhelmed to know what to add to your cart. Luckily, we're here to help. Read on to find the best Amazon Summer Beauty Event deals on products we recommend, including deals under $10. Keep in mind that the deals are updating daily, so prices are likely to change if you wait too long to shop.

    Amazon Summer Beauty Event frequently asked questions

    When does the Amazon Summer Beauty Event end?

    The Amazon Summer Beauty Event went live on April 28 and will run until May 11, so you still have some time to shop. But remember that there are special deals happening each day of the sale, so you may want to be strategic about which days to shop.

    Do I need to be a Prime member to shop the Amazon Summer Beauty Event?

    You don't have to have a Prime membership to shop deals from the Amazon Summer Beauty Event. That said, Prime members can enjoy other perks like free shipping and delivery on all orders. If you're interested in signing up, learn more on Amazon's website.
