    Bedding deal FAQ

    Is Prime Day a good time to buy bedding?

    Based on our research, now is an excellent time to refresh your bedding, especially pillows, throws, sheets, and comforters. Most deals convincing our editors to buy are discounts in the 20-40% off range, and we use price checkers like CamelCamelCamel and Keepa to confirm its lowest historical price or the last time it was discounted. However, not all brands offer their best deals or temporarily inflate prices ahead of Prime Day to make it seem like a great deal. For instance, we regularly see the Tempur-Pedic Tempur-Adapt mattress topper at a discount, but we've seen even better prices on it than what's on offer for Prime Day.

    What other bedding brands are having sales during Prime Day?

    Several other popular bedding brands are participating in the Prime Day discounts on their respective websites. For instance, The Company Store is offering up to 40% off; Cozy Earth is offering 30% off sitewide; both Boll and Branch and Buffy are giving 20% off everything; and PeachSkinSheets are just $85 when you add the code PRIME at checkout.
