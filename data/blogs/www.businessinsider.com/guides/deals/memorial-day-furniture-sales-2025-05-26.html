    Memorial Day is one of the best times of year to buy furniture, especially if you're looking to fill out your outdoor space or living room. Retailers of every price point celebrate the long weekend by offering great holiday deals on couches, outdoor sets, dining tables, beds, and much more. That's why I've gathered the best Memorial Day furniture sales for you to browse below.

    If you've been waiting to buy a new piece for your home, don't hesitate. Many of the best sales are ending today, and the next major sales event likely won't arrive until Amazon Prime Day comes in the summer. Rather than pay full price for these big-ticket items, act on these Memorial Day deals from stores like Revival, CB2, Pottery Barn, Amazon, and Wayfair before they expire.

    Keep scrolling to find the best furniture deals the holiday has to offer, including my top five discounts from the event overall. For more of today's savings, check out our roundup of the best Memorial Day sales here, including apparel, TVs, appliances, and more.

    Are Memorial Day furniture sales going on in person?

    Many retailers like Anthropologie, Ballard Designs, and more are offering Memorial Day deals both online and in-store. But keep in mind that the in-store selection may be limited or sell out faster, so you may have better luck online. Some brands are also offering online exclusive deals that may be more enticing than what you'll find in person.

    When do Memorial Day furniture sales end?

    Every retailer has a different end date for its sale, but many will end shortly after the holiday, which falls on Monday, May 26. Also, keep in mind that certain items, colors, or fabrics are likely to sell out before the sale actually ends, so getting your shopping in early is your best bet.

    Are Memorial Day furniture sales worth it?

    Memorial Day furniture deals offer some of the best discounts we see all year, rivaled only by the ones we see during Black Friday. Now's a great time to shop, especially if you want to refresh your outdoor furniture setup in time for summer.
