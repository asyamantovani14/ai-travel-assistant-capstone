    Independence Day sales are live, but as the day ends, so do they. We've rounded up all the best 4th of July deals in one place. This year's offerings are varied, with furniture (both indoors and outdoors), mattresses, camping, clothing, makeup, and tech stores all getting in on the action.

    Even with Amazon Prime Day just around the corner on July 16 - which might have better deals on things like TVs, tech, and gaming - you'll find the selected Independence Day sales below are a great opportunity to find products on sale from direct-to-consumer and startup brands. These are likely items we won't see on Prime Day. If you're shopping for furniture, decor, apparel, or outdoor gear, now is a good time to do so. 

    Below, you can find everything from my top five 4th of July deals to a list of sales from retailers we trust and love. For even more savings, be sure to check out our roundups to the best Independence Day mattress sales and deals on products made in the USA.
