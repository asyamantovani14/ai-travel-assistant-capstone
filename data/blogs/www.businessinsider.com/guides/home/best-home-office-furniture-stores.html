The 12 best home office furniture stores to shop online

Setting up a home office can take a lot of planning, especially when it comes to choosing functional and attractive furniture. Nikki Klugh, an award-winning interior designer and owner of Nikki Klugh Design Group, shared some of her favorite stores for home office furniture as well as tips on how to personalize what she dubs "a money space."

"A money space is an area in your home that immediately activates you, makes you more productive, efficient, and profitable in your home office," Klugh says.

Whether you're an entrepreneur building your business from home, a teacher grading papers on nights and weekends, or a programmer with a hybrid schedule, choosing the right furniture for your home office will help you create an enjoyable, functional space. 

We've tested plenty of home office furniture and accessories, from ergonomic chairs to standing desks. Based on our expertise and the advice of Klugh, we compiled this list of the best home office furniture stores for a range of budgets and aesthetics, from affordable to high-end and traditional to midcentury modern.

Wayfair

Best for: Contemporary, traditional, minimalist aesthetic

Product range: Desks, office chairs, modular sets, shelves, bookcases, laptop stands, filing cabinets

Shipping costs: Free shipping over $35

Average shipping time: 8 business days

White glove delivery: Yes, price varies by furniture size

Return policy: Within 30 days

Return fees: Fee will be deducted from refund and depends on where you live

Wayfair has a vast selection of affordable furniture and accessories suitable for lightly used or temporary home office spaces. It's also ideal for college students looking for simple desks, laptop stands, and chairs. Website filters make it easier to sift through options for home office furniture and accessories, which range in price from $35 to $500. 

Wayfair doesn't manufacture its product offerings. Instead, it sells furniture from a variety of suppliers, which means the quality of items can vary. Checking product reviews on the website is always recommended, but Wayfair's return policy covers flawed furniture as well as pieces you change your mind about.

Klugh likes Wayfair for its selection of affordable statement and accent pieces. "A decorative room divider could be used to separate or close off spaces and help make you feel more focused," she says.

Amazon

Best for: Modern, traditional, basic furniture

Product range: Chairs, desks, storage, shelves, bookcases, laptop stands

Shipping costs: Varies by seller

Average shipping time: Can range from same day to 21 days

White glove delivery: No

Return policy: Varies by seller

Return fees: Varies by seller

Whether you're shopping for luxury home office furniture or affordable pieces with a shorter lifespan, Amazon has something for everyone. You'll also find items from some of our favorite brands, including Flexispot, Steelcase, Herman Miller, and Fully.

You can find a variety of sturdy ergonomic chairs, large standing desks, desks for small corners, and any kind of storage to fit your home office. Unlike Wayfair, Amazon also has a private-label brand, Amazon Basics. Products under this label aren't necessarily manufactured by Amazon but are good budget-friendly options if you're looking to save some money. 

Be aware that items sold through third-party sellers may present issues, so you should carefully read both product and seller reviews before placing an order.

Pottery Barn

Best for: Transitional, farmhouse aesthetic

Product range: Desks, office chairs, shelves, bookcases, filing cabinets

Shipping costs:10% of the total cost of purchases $200 and over, surcharge may be added for bigger items 

Average shipping time: 7 to 10 business days

White glove delivery: Yes, $159 to $259

Return policy: Return within 30 days of receiving or purchasing an item, custom furniture is non-returnable

Return fees: None

Pottery Barn is a well-known furniture store that offers in-store and online shopping for high-quality home office furniture. Although Pottery Barn is on the more expensive side, especially for bulkier pieces like desks and bookcases, there are plenty of office chairs, wall shelves, and accessories at moderate prices. 

Klugh frequently uses pieces from Pottery Barn to design commercial and residential offices because of its selection of luxury furniture, upholstery fabrics, useful task lighting, and classic designs. 

In addition, Pottery Barn sells ADA-compliant office furniture as part of their Accessible Home collection. The selection includes functional designs for those who require extra mobility, easier access, and more comfort. For an accessible home office, Klugh also recommends removing rugs and carpets and moving furniture to create a more open space.

Fully

Best for: Simple, functional furniture

Product range: Desks, chairs, storage

Shipping costs: Free shipping in the contiguous US

Average shipping time: Within 1 to 7 business days

White glove delivery: No

Return policy: Within 30 to 90 days, depending on product, though some items are non-returnable

Return fees: Free

Working from home can mean hours of sitting or standing in the same position. If you're looking for highly functional adjustable desks and comfortable office chairs, we recommend Fully. The certified B corp is committed to making budget-friendly minimalist office furniture with sustainable practices and materials.

In addition to some of our favorite office chairs and standing desks, Fully also has a selection of storage and space dividers, desk systems, storage solutions, and desktop accessories. Much of its furniture can be coordinated to create a cohesive home office.

Fully's products are made from several materials, including steel, recycled wood, and sustainable bamboo. They come packaged in recyclable materials and the company donates unsellable returned furniture to nonprofit organizations throughout Portland, Oregon.

West Elm

Best for: Midcentury modern designs

Product range: Desks, office chairs, shelves, bookcases, filing cabinets

Shipping costs: Varies depending on merchandise total; orders exceeding $300 have a shipping cost of 10% of the merchandise total 

Average shipping time: Ships within 4 to 5 business days

White glove delivery: Yes, for $159 to $409 depending on the price and distance

Return policy: Within 30 days, with the exception of made-to-order, personalized, and final sale items

Return fees: Varies, contact customer service for additional details

One of the more well-known home goods and furniture stores, West Elm sells reasonably priced midcentury-modern-inspired furniture. Its home office offerings include modular systems, office chairs, shelving, filing cabinets, and desks ranging from L-shape designs to simple writing tables. The pieces are on trend yet neutral enough to blend in with existing furniture and decor.

Much of West Elm's furniture is constructed from renewable woods, medium-density fiberboard, and metal. This makes it more affordable than high-end brands that use solid hardwoods like oak and maple. 

West Elm has various brick-and-mortar locations across the country so you can see items in person before purchasing. In-store purchases are also eligible for flat-rate home delivery.

Herman Miller

Best for: Modern, contemporary furnishings

Product range: Chairs, desks, shelves, filing cabinets, desk lamps

Shipping costs: FedEx is free for orders over $100; in-home delivery is between 12% of order value and $599, depending on distance

Average shipping time: Within 1 to 5 business days

White glove delivery: Yes, $399 to $599

Return policy: Within 30 days of receipt or date of purchase, whichever is later

Return fees: Restocking fee of 10% of the total purchase price up to $500 per item 

When people think of luxury and high-end ergonomic office furniture, Herman Miller often comes to mind. From standing desks and filing cabinets to the iconic Aeron chair, the company is a great option for those who prioritize next-level comfort, adjustability, and support when working from home for long hours. 

While many customers say that certain pieces such as desk chairs aren't as attractive as those of other furniture brands, their quality and durability make them worthy of consideration.

For those whose home office doubles as a gaming space, Herman Miller's selection of gaming chairs, desks, and accessories have sleek designs with relaxing yet efficient features.

Lulu and Georgia

Best for: Modern, bohemian, vintage-inspired, eclectic designs

Product range: Desks, chairs, cabinets, bookcases, desk lamps, desk accessories

Shipping costs: Varies depending on merchandise total; orders exceeding $220 have a shipping cost of 10% of the pre-discounted merchandise total

Average shipping time: 14 to 18 business days

White glove delivery: Yes, $199 to $369

Return policy: Within 60 days

Return fees: None

If you're looking for vintage-inspired or modern home office furniture and decorative objects, Lulu and Georgia is an excellent choice. Its selection of home office furniture, while on the pricier side, includes beautifully designed office chairs, unique filing cabinets, and modern desks with a rustic touch. 

Lulu and Georgia also has an eclectic selection of wall art, which Klugh says is essential for personalizing your home office and making it cohesive. Despite the slightly higher prices in comparison to stores like Pottery Barn and Crate and Barrel, Lulu and Georgia has an abundant sale section with decorative items for your home office like rugs, lamps, and vases.

Due to carrier shipping constraints, Lulu and Georgia has been facing several shipping delays, especially for large items like furniture.

Branch

Best for: Contemporary furniture

Product range: Desks, chairs, filing cabinets, desk accessories

Shipping costs: Free for most orders

Average shipping time: Within 1 to 3 business days

White glove delivery: White glove delivery and white glove installation available for certain items

Return policy: Returned with original packaging within 30 days

Return fees: Shipping fees apply

Branch has a limited selection of contemporary home office furniture, but its chairs are noteworthy. The attractive and affordable Ergonomic Chair landed in the top spot in our guide to the best ergonomic office chairs. We like its range of adjustability and all-day comfort.

Branch's chairs are made for a range of body types and needs. On its website, you can shop by support needs, including lumbar support, active work, and better posture. 

You'll also find a variety of desks, including standing desks, L-desks, and desks for larger spaces like conference rooms. Its small selection of desk accessories, filing cabinets, and credenzas is worth checking out too.

Staples

Best for: Corporate, modern, traditional

Product range: Chairs, desks, bookcases, shelves, filing cabinets

Shipping costs: Free shipping over $30

Average shipping time: Within 1 to 2 business days; most furniture is available for next-day delivery

White glove delivery: Yes, $80

Return policy: Within 14 days

Return fees: None

Staples may be best known for its basic office supplies, but it also carries a wide variety of comfortable office chairs, desks, and versatile storage solutions. In fact, it can be a one-stop shop for outfitting your home office.

You'll find a mix of affordable and high-end pieces in a range of styles, including contemporary, corporate, academic, and traditional. We highly recommend Staples if you're shopping for an office chair.

While the selection is limited in its brick-and-mortar stores, there are a handful of chairs you can test out in person. You can also browse online and easily filter hundreds of products from brands like La-Z-Boy, Tempur-Pedic, and Hon.

Article

Best for: Contemporary and midcentury modern styles

Product range: Chairs, desks, bookcases, shelves, cabinets, lighting

Shipping costs: Items under $200 have a $19 shipping fee, items $201 to $999 have a $49 fee, and items $999+ ship free

Average shipping time: Varies by item

White glove delivery: Yes, $119

Return policy: Within 30 days

Return fees: $19 to $49 depending on the service area

The direct-to-consumer furniture brand Article has grown quite popular since its launch in 2013. We appreciate its ready-to-ship furniture as well as customizable pieces in contemporary and midcentury-modern designs. 

Article's desks and office chairs include a variety of attractive contemporary designs at very reasonable prices. Its bookcases, cabinets, and desk lighting are also priced somewhat below brands like West Elm and Crate and Barrel. 

Many Insider Reviews editors and reporters own furniture from Article that they rave about. While assembly isn't always as quick as the brand promises, the end result is sturdy and beautiful furniture that might make your home office the best-looking room in your home.

The Container Store

Best for: Modern aesthetic

Product range: Cabinets, chairs, desks, desk accessories, drawer organizers, modular sets

Shipping costs: Items more than $75 ship free; extremely heavy items may incur additional fees

Average shipping time: Within 3 to 5 business days

White glove delivery: Most custom Elfa systems include delivery and installation

Return policy: Within 120 days

Return fees: None

Despite its name, The Container Store offers more than home storage solutions. Its selection of filing cabinets, modular systems, and desktop accessories can add to your home office's functionality. 

While its selection of desks and chairs is more limited than other stores in this article, The Container Store's storage solutions and organizers are great supplemental items. With free design services, in-home organizers, and installation services, you can transform your home office into a well-organized space.

The Container Store's loyalty and rewards program, called Organized Insider, also offers plenty of savings, special offers, extra discounts, and receipt-free returns. Even without joining the program, The Container Store's selection is mostly affordable with options for customization. With dozens of locations across the country, it's also easy to shop in-store.

Crate & Barrel

Best for: Classic, modern styles

Product range: Desks, chairs, bookshelves, shelves, filing cabinets, accessories 

Shipping costs: Varies depending on merchandise total; orders over $220 have a shipping cost of 10% of the merchandise total plus $24.95

Average shipping time: 3 to 5 business days 

White glove delivery: Varies by location

Return policy: Contact Crate & Barrel within 7 days of purchase or delivery and return within 30 days in-store

Return fees: Not applicable for in-store returns; shipping and handling fees for non-furniture items are non-refundable

Crate & Barrel is known for higher-end home office furniture, from desks and chairs to filing cabinets and bookcases. If you have a more generous budget, it's an excellent place to start designing your space. With free design services and white glove delivery, you can simplify the process of furnishing your home office.

Many of Crate & Barrel's pieces include a combination of hardwood and veneers, like this white oak desk, but you'll also find affordable accessories like a felt letterboard. Klugh recommends a bulletin board to keep track of important notes and goals.

With several brick-and-mortar locations throughout the country, you can also check out furniture in person.
