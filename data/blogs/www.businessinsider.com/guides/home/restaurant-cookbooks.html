50 cookbooks from famous restaurants across the US that will help you recreate their best dishes at home

  • Restaurant cookbooks provide an intimate glimpse into a professional chef's inspiration and process, as well as the opportunity to "visit" a dining establishment regardless of physical distance. 
  • If you're missing your favorite restaurant right now or want to discover a new one from the comfort of your kitchen, you'll find it in these 50 diverse cookbook options. 
  • Recipe ideas range from creative Chinese-American fusion to historic and authentic Spanish cuisine to nostalgia-fueled ice cream treats.
  • See also: 11 cookbooks any home chef would find useful

Having lived in Los Angeles, the Bay Area, and now New York City, I've always been surrounded by amazing food options. And I've found that restaurant cookbooks are a great way to remember — and recreate — particularly excellent dining experiences. Since it's rare to be able to meet the chefs behind your favorite meals, much less ask them about their recipe-developing processes, their cookbooks give you an opportunity to peek into their brains.

Restaurant cookbooks are also a great way to try dishes you've only pined after online, whether the spot you've been dying to visit is a beloved neighborhood eatery, a famous dessert shop, or a Michelin-starred establishment. While some of these books contain recipes that are quite involved for the regular home cook, at the very least they provide lots of culinary inspiration. And right now, with restaurants closed or limited to takeout and delivery due to the coronavirus, they'll help you feel closer to a city's dining scene. 

The 50 cookbooks below come from some of America's best and most beloved restaurants and bakeries, and truly represent the country's diverse tastes. 

Tartine

"Tartine," $25.23, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Lines go out the door no matter what time of the day or week you visit this San Francisco bakery. Just like you can't decide on any one pastry to buy, you won't be able to choose which recipe to make first: Will it be the Brioche Bread Pudding, Lemon Cream Tart, or Chocolate Chiffon Cake? 

 

Shake Shack

"Shake Shack: Recipes & Stories," $20.88, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Before you get too excited, no, this book does not contain the actual recipes for the burger or the famous sauce, but it will get you pretty close to the real deal. You can make delicious burgers, fries, and shakes at home, then conduct the classic Shake Shack vs. In-n-Out comparison. 

 

Sqirl

"Everything I Want to Eat: Sqirl and the New California Cooking," $25.49, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

With its colorful, fresh, and health-conscious dishes, Sqirl captures every stereotype you've heard about Los Angeles and its obsession with clean eating. You'll see kale, avocado, and grain bowls alongside unique jams, sophisticated cookies, and elevated beverages. 

 

Alinea

"Alinea," $48.52, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

This distinctive three Michelin-starred Chicago restaurant helped put molecular gastronomy on the map — meaning, recipes can skew a bit complicated. That being said, the book also comes with access to a website featuring video demonstrations, interviews, and an online forum that allows readers to interact with co-owner Grant Achatz and his team.

 

Momofuku Milk Bar

"Momofuku Milk Bar," $24.63, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Christina Tosi's creative desserts play on your childhood favorites, from the Compost Cookie and Crack Pie to the famous Cereal Milk Ice Cream and "unfinished" layer cakes. If you're looking for fun indulgence, this cookbook is it. 

 

Dominique Ansel

"Dominique Ansel: The Secret Recipes," $24.49, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

You know him for the Cronut that took New York and the rest of the world by storm, but Ansel also makes plenty of other delicious pastries. The book is split into three sections by experience level, so you can move through madeleines, macarons, and Kouign-Amanns as you see fit. 

Chez Panisse

"Chez Panisse Vegetables," $36.99, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

It's impossible to talk about California cuisine without mentioning Alice Waters. Fresh, seasonal vegetables are the name of the game and are essential to the menu at her Berkeley restaurant. These recipes will quickly turn you into a farmer's market regular. 

 

The French Laundry

"The French Laundry Cookbook," $52.67, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

This three Michelin-starred Napa Valley restaurant has been dazzling visitors with its two nine-course tasting menus for more than two decades. Learn from chef and proprietor Thomas Keller how to make the refined and focused dishes that made The French Laundry so famous. 

 

Lemonade

"The Lemonade Cookbook: Southern California Comfort Food from L.A.'s Favorite Modern Cafeteria," $9.64, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Pair one of these salads, sandwiches, or stews with a tart, fruity lemonade, and you have a lunch that will make all your coworkers jealous. Fans of this Los Angeles staple will have fun recreating their favorite dishes. 

State Bird Provisions

"State Bird Provisions: A Cookbook," $26.49, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

The dishes are brought to you dim sum service-style at this trendy San Francisco restaurant, so you'll fill up your table with a variety of delicious flavors and textures before you know it. Try your hand at Fried Pork Belly, Spicy Yuba Noodles, Sweet Corn Tres Leches Cake, and more in your own home kitchen.

 

Eleven Madison Park

"Eleven Madison Park: The Cookbook," $46.31, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

125 sophisticated recipes fill this book from the iconic New York restaurant. Woven throughout the recipes is the story of how the successful establishment came to be. 

Mission Chinese Food

"The Mission Chinese Food Cookbook," $39.99, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Chinese-American food is a distinct category in itself, no better captured than by Danny Bowien's fusion dishes like Kung Pao Pastrami and Thrice-Cooked Bacon. 

 

Grand Central Market

"The Grand Central Market Cookbook: Cuisine and Culture from Downtown Los Angeles," $20.99, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

This legendary food hall features a melting pot of vendors — whatever you're craving, Grand Central Market has it. Make some carnitas tacos, curry udon, and pressed juice, then settle in to learn about the market's rich history. 

 

The Cheese Board

"The Cheese Board: Collective Works: Bread, Pastry, Cheese, Pizza," $22.99, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes & Noble

In 2016, the highest-rated pizza place in America according to Yelp was Cheese Board, which produces only one type of vegetarian pizza a day and also makes fresh bread and cheese right next door. This book of over 100 recipes is the ultimate carb party. 

 

Cúrate

"Cúrate: Authentic Spanish Food from an American Kitchen," $31.66, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Some of the best Spanish food in the country can be found in Asheville, North Carolina at tapas bar Cúrate. Chef Katie Button dropped out of a PhD program to pursue her passion for cooking, and thousands of satisfied diners have thanked her since. 

 

Gjelina

"Gjelina: Cooking from Venice, California," $23.27, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Accessible ingredients make up the rustic dishes of this Californian-Mediterranean restaurant. You'll find a lot of roasted and grilled vegetables, interesting salads, and slow-cooked meats. 

 

Momofuku

"Momofuku," $35.99, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

David Chang serves up modern Asian cuisine in his collection of award-winning restaurants. If you like noodles and pork, you'll love his tribute to the comforting ingredients. 

 

The Gramercy Tavern

"The Gramercy Tavern Cookbook," $35.19, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

If you can't snag a reservation at this New York institution, the cookbook brings you right into its dining room and kitchen. Feast on Fresh Spinach Fettuccine, Duck Confit, and Roasted Almond Panna Cotta. 

 

Myers+Chang

"Myers+Chang at Home: Recipes from the Beloved Boston Eatery," $19.99, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

The marriage of chef Joanne Chang and restaurateur Christopher Myers resulted in a child called Myers+Chang, an Asian fusion restaurant in Boston that brings the best of both their worlds together. Think Grilled Corn with Spicy Sriracha Butter, Braised Bulgogi BBQ Sloppy Joes, and other cool combinations. 

Black Tap

"Craft Burgers and Crazy Shakes from Black Tap," $10.99, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

The camera definitely comes first at this restaurant made for Instagrammable moments. The hearty burgers and stacked milkshakes are the epitome of outrageous indulgence. 

 

Bianco

"Bianco: Pizza, Pasta, and Other Food I Like," $34.99, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Who doesn't love a good slice of pizza? You'd never guess that this thriving pizzeria in Phoenix, Arizona started inside the back corner of a grocery store. 

 

The Slanted Door

"The Slanted Door: Modern Vietnamese Food," $30.99, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Classic Vietnamese dishes take on an American twist at The Slanted Door, which remains on many San Francisco visitors' lists for its tasty home-inspired food and beautiful location on the waterfront. 

 

Nopalito

"Nopalito: A Mexican Kitchen," $22.30, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Nopalito perfectly captures authentic, regional Mexican food, offering fundamental techniques alongside insights into Mexican culture. 

 

Del Posto

"The Del Posto Cookbook," $25.04, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

The recipes in this Italian cookbook are accompanied by photography inspired by 16th century still life paintings, confirming that food is indeed a form of art. 

 

Bäco Mercat

"Baco: Vivid Recipes from the Heart of Los Angeles," $35, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Chef Josef Centeno shares recipes from all five of his Historic Core restaurants: Bäco Mercat, Bar Amá, Orsa & Winston, Ledlow, and P.Y.T. Multicultural flavors shine in this visually captivating book. 

 

Burma Superstar

"Burma Superstar: Addictive Recipes from the Crossroads of Southeast Asia," $20.39, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

The Tea Leaf Salad is a must-try at this Burmese restaurant. Burmese food draws influences from India, China, Thailand, and Laos, so expect lots of savory spice. 

 

Ample Hills Creamery

"Ample Hills Creamery: Secrets and Stories from Brooklyn's Favorite Ice Cream Shop," $18.29, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Put your ice cream machine to work with this fun book full of recipes for everyone's favorite frozen treat. We always have room for dessert if it means eating Ample Hills Creamery's Salted Crack Caramel ice cream. 

 

Prune

"Prune," $38.05, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

This cookbook is great for home cooks who want to feel like they're working in a professional kitchen. The inside is designed like the actual kitchen binders used in Prune, and written in a voice intended for line cooks. Along with other famous recipes from the NYC Lower East Side establishment, there's an entire chapter on how to make "garbage" and kitchen sink bits delicious. 

Balthazar

"The Balthazar Cookbook," $34.46, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

For a taste of Paris, New Yorkers can simply go to this French brasserie to enjoy classic French dishes like Bouillabaisse and Macaroni Gratin. Even if you can't dine al fresco in Soho, you can enjoy the same food in your own dining room. 

Laurel

"Laurel: Modern American Flavors in Philidelphia," $22.99, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Get ready to create full nine-course tasting menus with this cookbook, written by chef-owner and "Top Chef" winner Nicholas Elmi. The South Philly restaurant is known for its creative flavors, plating, and intimate dining space. 

Homeroom

"The Mac + Cheese Cookbook," $15.97, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

At Homeroom in Oakland, California, kids and adults alike pack tables to enjoy a menu of all different types of mac and cheese, from Truffle Mac to Jalapeño Popper Mac. If you're a fan of comfort food, you'll love these cheesy, decadent recipes. 

Rao's

"Rao's Classics: More than 140 Italian Favorites from the Legendary New York Restaurant," $31.51, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

It's notoriously difficult to snag a seat at Rao's, but this cookbook is a great alternative. There are more than 140 recipes for Italian classics like eggplant parm and margherita pizza. 

Johnny Harris

"Johnny Harris Restaurant Cookbook," $26.95, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Bakers have plenty of opportunities to flex their pastry skills with recipes like Chocolate Caramel Thumbprint Tartlets and Brown Butter Shortbread. The book is also just beautiful to flip through, featuring vivid, full-sized photos that capture the desserts in all their glory. 

Tujague's

"Tujague's Cookbook: Creole Recipes and Lore in the New Orleans Grand Traditions," $24.99, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

"Tujague's Cookbook" masterfully weaves history through its recipes, providing rich insight into the second oldest restaurant in New Orleans. You'll feel satiated by the memorabilia from the restaurant's archives, ghost stories, and well-researched tidbits. 

Zuni Cafe

"The Zuni Cafe Cookbook: A Compendium of Recipes and Cooking Lessons from San Francisco's Beloved Restaurant," $24.49, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Chef-owner Judy Rodgers got her start as a lunch chef at Chez Panisse before opening her very popular restaurant Zuni Cafe in San Francisco, where fresh, seasonal ingredients and simple recipes reign supreme. The juicy, crispy Roast Chicken, made using only small and organic chickens, is a favorite. 

Columbia Restaurant

"The Columbia Restaurant Spanish Cookbook," $11.94, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Columbia Restaurant holds the distinction of being both the oldest restaurant in Florida and the biggest Spanish restaurant in the US. It doesn't merely ride the coattails of its historical significance and remains relevant by offering delicious, authentic dishes (e.g. Spanish bean soup, arroz con pollo) that reviewers and diners fall in love with instantly. 

Ox

"Around the Fire: Recipes for Inspired Grilling and Seasonal Feasting from Ox Restaurant," $35, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

With summer around the corner, you'll feel inspired to up your outdoor grilling game with the help of Ox chefs Greg Denton and Gabrielle Quiñónez Denton. Don't be afraid to crank up the fire for dishes like Grilled Wild Halibut on the Bone with Toasted Garlic-Lemon Oil. 

Moosewood

"The Moosewood Restaurant Table: 250 Brand -New Recipes from the Natural Foods Restaurant That Revolutionized Eating in America," $22.99, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Moosewood is proof is that there are many ways to have fun with vegetables and other healthy ingredients — Butternut Latkes, Cashew-Crusted Chickpea Burgers, and Orange Pistachio Cornmeal Cake are on the menu, along with recipes to please vegans and gluten-free cooks. 

Market Restaurant + Bar

"Market Restaurant + Bar Cookbook: Seasonally Inspired Cuisine from Southern California," $30.23, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Local ingredients and fresh seafood feature prominently at Market Restaurant + Bar in Del Mar, California. In the book, Carl Schroeder splits recipes up by season, so you're always making the best of your grocery haul. 

The Stinking Rose

"The Stinking Rose Restaurant Cookbook," $15.99, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Garlic lovers won't be able to resist the tantalizing recipes from Stinking Rose, which serves more than 3,000 pounds of garlic each month. Make sure both your garlic and breath mint supply are fully stocked in preparation to cook these dishes. 

Clinton St. Baking Company

"Clinton St. Baking Company Cookbook: Breakfast, Brunch & Beyond from New York's Favorite Neighborhood Restaurant," $52.92, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble (Nook Book only)

Clinton St. Baking Company has mastered the art of brunch. You can trust its guidance for sweet and savory Sunday morning favorites from pancakes to crab cakes. 

Foreign Cinema

"The Foreign Cinema Cookbook: Recipes and Stories Under the Stars," $27.95, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

You can't help but fall in love under the romantic lights at Foreign Cinema, which shows a film every night in the airy atrium to accompany its simple and beautiful food. The menu is part North African, part California-Mediterranean, telling stories just like the films on screen. 

Various restaurants and bars in Portland, Oregon

"Portland Cooks: Recipes from the City's Best Restaurants and Bars," $20.49, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Rather than focus on one specific restaurant, "Portland Cooks" shows you 80 recipes from 40 of Stumptown's diverse culinary scene. It's like a sampler cookbook of sorts, representing Japanese, Spanish, Greek, Moroccan, classic American, and even more types of cuisines. 

Phoenicia Diner

"The Phoenicia Diner Cookbook: Dishes and Dispatches from the Catskill Mountains," $22.49, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Phoenicia Diner is nestled in the Catskills and offers classic diner food with a modern twist like Cider-Braised Duck and Grits or Twice-Baked Potato Skillets. It brings all the comforts of a beloved local diner to your own kitchen. 

Roberta's

"Roberta's Cookbook," $35, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Those who live around Bushwick have fond memories of Roberta's, a pizza joint that stands out among the sea of pizza options in New York City. Beyond pizza, there are recipes for meat, vegetables, and pasta, so there's something for everyone. 

Emily

"Emily: The Cookbook," $17.84, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

The burger that has topped many "best of" lists comes from a surprising source: a pizza restaurant. After you've filled yourself with the famous pretzel bun-topped burger and thick Detroit-style pizza, move on to the strong cocktails and rich desserts. 

Jack's Wife Freda

"Jack's Wife Freda: Cooking From New York's West Village," $17.01, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

There are too many memorable dishes to count from Jack's Wife Freda, which features Jewish home cooking with South African and Israeli flavors. You can never go wrong with a comforting Matzoh Ball Soup, nor the Green Shakshuka and Rosewater Waffles with Honey Syrup. 

Ivan Ramen

"Ivan Ramen: Love, Obsession, and Recipes from Tokyo's Most Unlikely Noodle Joint," $19.59, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

To understand why Ivan Ramen's bowl of noodles is so good, you first need to get inside the head and story of chef Ivan Orkin. He's not shy about sharing his complete recipe for the famous Shio Ramen, in addition to important add-ons like Chashu Pork and Half-Cooked Eggs.

Hawker Fare

"Hawker Fare: Stories & Recipes from a Refugee Chef's Isan Thai & Lao Roots," $26.01, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Following chef James Syhabout's fine dining experiment Commis, Hawker Fare takes a different direction, inspired by the colorful, flavorful foods served at open-air markets in Thailand and Laos. After learning the basics of pantry staples, you can move on to dishes like Lao Pho and Minced Pork Salad.

Little Flower Cafe

"Little Flower: Recipes from the Cafe," $20.59, available at Amazon

Also available at: Barnes and Noble

Bakers have plenty of opportunities to flex their pastry skills with recipes like Chocolate Caramel Thumbprint Tartlets and Brown Butter Shortbread. The book is also just beautiful to flip through, featuring vivid, full-sized photos that capture the desserts in all their glory.
