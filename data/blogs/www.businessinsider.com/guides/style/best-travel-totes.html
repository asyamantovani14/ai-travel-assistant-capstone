The 10 best travel totes that fit under an airplane seat and carry everything you need

I travel frequently for both work and leisure, and if there's one thing I've learned, it's this: While I can't control potential delays, lost luggage, or screaming children in my row, I can make the experience less stressful and more enjoyable by packing a well-equipped carry-on for the flight. That means having a trusty travel tote that can accommodate my books, earbuds, and laptop so I have plenty of entertainment options — as well as my favorite snacks to fuel me through.

The best travel totes, as far as I'm concerned, are roomy enough to fit all my must-have items, but also comfortable enough to lug around as I'm hustling from one gate to the next. And I'd rather not compromise on style, either. Just as I began to wonder: "Is this too much to ask?" — I found a handful of holy grail totes that I keep reaching for again and again.

What we looked for in a travel tote

Travel totes come in many shapes, materials, and sizes, and with a range of storage features. While evaluating a number of options, there were several key elements I looked for.

First, I tested how easy it would be to carry the bag through an airport. A luggage sleeve is ideal because it allows you to attach the tote onto a wheelie bag, but that feature is harder to find on a tote and it's not make or break for me personally. However, I did make sure the tote could feasibly sit on top of a suitcase while I rolled it, or at the very least was comfortable enough to wear on my shoulder for long stretches.

Second, I made sure it could easily fit all my travel essentials. I considered what types of items I typically travel with — like a water bottle, laptop, a few snacks, etc —and whether the bag would not only accommodate them space-wise but also make them easy to find.

I also factored in whether the bag could feasibly fit under the seat in front during take-off and landing. It would be so annoying to be told to put your bag in an overhead compartment when you need to access your essentials throughout the flight.

Lastly, and most importantly, I only tested zip-top tote bags. The last thing anyone needs while traveling is a lost passport or wallet, and the zipper closure makes it so much easier to keep things secure.

It works for under-seat storage. And despite how much it can hold, this tote still fits right under the seat in front of me during take-off and landing — plus, the handles fold down, making it even easier to stash.

Best budget: BAGSMART The Wanderland Travel Tote

Check price at Bagsmart

It's remarkably affordable. At only $36.90, this tote definitely doesn't break the bank. That means more funds to spend on souvenirs, memorable meals, and other travel-related luxuries.

There are ample pockets. I have a hard time staying organized, but the Bagsmart Wanderland Travel Tote definitely makes it a bit easier. There are multiple zipper and slip pockets for all my belongings, a dedicated padded laptop sleeve (which fits up to a 15.6" device), and external zip pouches on the front and back. I use the hidden back pocket with zipper closure to store my keys, phone, and wallet so they're easy to access yet totally secure.

It's easy to care for. I've been hard-pressed to find the best travel tote that you can also toss in the washing machine, but this one fits the bill. If you don't want to fully wash it, you can also spot clean it with a damp cloth. This is a major bonus since my bag can get pretty grimy during travel.

One pro tip: The quilted cotton fabric does seem to sometimes attract lint, hair, etc. — so if that bothers you, consider buying any color other than black so it's less noticeable.

Best lightweight: BUG Lightweight Women’s Laptop Tote Bag

It's remarkably roomy. At first glance, this tote looked pretty modest in size — but I was shocked at just how much I could squeeze in there, including my laptop, tablet, book, planner, umbrella, external charging port, sunglasses, wallet, and even a light sweater. Note: the padded laptop compartment can fit a device up to 15.6".

It's machine washable. Since the BUG Lightweight Tote is 100% cotton, I can also throw it into my washing machine on a gentle cycle.

It's incredibly lightweight. It's pretty important to me that my travel bags are lightweight — I'm not looking to make carrying my belongings around any more strenuous. This tote fits the bill.

Check price at MZ Wallace

It's convertible. As a general rule, I love having options. So, I appreciate that this tote comes with a detachable and adjustable crossbody strap, as well as a luggage sleeve for when I'd rather unburden my shoulders and attach it to my rolling suitcase.

There are so many color options. The ultra-popular MZ Wallace Metro Tote comes in a whopping 21 different colorways, from classic neutrals like black, pearl white, and walnut brown to fun alternatives like cherry red, emerald green, and metallic copper. Literally, there's an option for every mood, vibe, and travel fit.

The pockets are plentiful. Organization definitely isn't an issue with this tote, which fits my 13" laptop perfectly. With six interior pockets and five exterior pockets, I never have to worry about digging into the depths of my bag to find my phone charger, lip balm, or whatever else I might need at any given moment.

It's really comfortable. There's nothing I despise more than the feeling of straps digging into my shoulders, and it's a common issue when I've packed a particularly heavy bag for a long flight. That's never been a problem with this tote, though, thanks to the reinforced and padded handles.

It's wide, deep, and soft-sided, so I'm honestly not sure there's anything you wouldn't be able to cram in here. I was super impressed that I was able to fit my laptop and charger, several books, a full change of clothes and shoes, snacks, a water bottle, and other smaller must-haves and somehow still had room for more.

I love that I can pack it inside my luggage, fill it with souvenirs on my trip, and then travel with it as a carry-on for the flight home.

It's machine washable. Let's be real: things just seem to get dirty quicker when you have kids (not that I would know from personal experience.) This tote, which is made with recycled nylon material, is not only machine washable but really easy to spot clean. When I accidentally spilled some coffee on the outside of the bag while racing to my gate, I was able to wipe it away in a cinch with just a moistened napkin.

The only thing that could probably improve this tote is some more internal pockets to keep things organized. That said, there is an exterior pocket for any items you need at the ready — like your phone and passport.

It's made to last. Parker Clay's products are handmade, and the company stands behind the quality — even offering a lifetime guarantee. Travel tote FAQs

What size travel tote fits under an airplane seat?

Though there is some variance based on airline, the standard dimensions for an under-the-seat bag are 18 inches by 14 inches by 8 inches.

Can a travel tote count as a personal item?

Yes, a travel tote will count as a personal item as long as it fits underneath the airplane seat. For more of our favorite picks, from backpacks to duffels, see our article on the best personal item bags.

What features should I look for in a good travel tote?

Some convenient travel tote features include:

  • A zipper across the entire top of the bag, so that items don't slip out when you slide the bag under the seat.
  • A luggage sleeve.
  • A secure pocket on the outside of the bag for quick access to passport, airline tickets, phone, etc.
  • Internal organization features like slip pockets, a key leash, and dividers so that you can easily access your items.
