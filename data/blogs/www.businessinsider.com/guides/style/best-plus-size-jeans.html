    Trying to find plus-size jeans that fit and are actually stylish used to be (and still is) an Olympic sport-level activity for fat people who just want a good pair of denim. Online and in-store availability has changed rapidly over the past five years. 

    Personally, I've seen the change in the industry as both a plus-size fashion shopper and a fat fashion writer who is obsessed with denim. Fashion and lifestyle content creator, Makayla Smith has noticed this change in the industry as well: "Now, with size expansion happening more rapidly, I'm able to find cuter and better fitting styles that help me to feel comfortable and let me further express myself."

    With the growing excitement of size expansions, there's a lot of discussion around finding the right fit as a fat person shopping for the best plus size clothing. To clarify what you should look for in the best plus size jeans, Insider tapped Alessandra Brunialti, SVP of Design at Lane Bryant, Celina Di Ciaula, Nordstrom Stylist, and Katie Murphy, Head of Fashion at 11 Honoré at Dia & Co.

    One of my favorite picks is this Wild Fable pair from Target, which is miraculously under $20. Or if your primary goal is to be comfy, these pull-on jeans from Good American are super stretchy and easy to wear. 

    Best overall: Universal Standard Farrah High Rise Flared Jeans

    Check price at Universal Standard

    • Size range: 00-40

    Universal Standard is the best of the best when it comes to inclusive size jeans and Insider has already put the brand to the test to prove that. You can't go wrong with any of their denim styles on their site, but these flared, high-rise jeans adda subtle 70's vibe that'll have you looking like a band member of Daisy Jones & The Six. Not to mention, they have deep pockets that can actually hold your phone and a comfortable stretch that moves with you.

    Plus, these jeans come in a distressed style with intentional tearing near the knee, as well as a true-blue and light wash option for various fashion choices. When you're ready to pick up a pair of one of the best plus-size jeans around, be sure to use Universal Standards specific size guide, the brand recommends having your waist measurements on hand to get the right fit.

    Best budget: Wild Fable Super-High Rise Distressed Skinny Jeans

    Check price at Target

    • Size range: 00-30

    Buying inexpensive and durable clothes that also look cute is still a hard feat in the world of plus-size shopping. Thankfully Target makes shopping on a budget fun and possible with brands like Wild Fable offering stylish, TikTok-worthy clothes like these distressed skinny jeans. Unlike lots of ultra-tight skinny jeans, these give you breathing room. Plus, the ripped jean style gives a fashionable flare that gives off a skater vibe without painful knee bruises. Fun Fact! Target has a pretty impressive return policy for their Target Owned Brand Items. In their words, if you're not satisfied, " return it within one year with a receipt for an exchange or a refund."

    Best high-waisted: Warp & Weft Mia Plus - High Rise Flare

    Check price at Warp & Weft

    • Size range: 16-24

    These high-rise flare jeans from Warp & Weft are one of my personal favorites because they hug my waist without leaving marks behind. I own several pairs of jeans from the brand, but I keep gravitating towards these for Karaoke nights with friends and grocery store trips because they're as comfortable as they are cute. Additionally, the subtle flare look contours my body to accentuate my figure with every walk down the street or run up transit stairs. 

    Bring the model-off-duty look to your wardrobe with these jeans and a simple bodysuit or crop top for a Bella Hadid-inspired outfit (Kin Euphorics is not necessary but a yummy treat!)

    Best short or petite: Lee Regular Fit Straight Leg Jean

    Check price at Amazon
    Check price at Kohl's
    Check price at Lee

    • Size range: 16-30

    The term "petite plus-size" is a phrase used to describe small-in-height plus-size folk who are big in the stomach, hips, thighs, etc. If you're petite plus and over your pants being too long in the leg or not wide enough in the waist/hip, then you're going to want to save these Lee jeans in your Amazon cart. 

    What sets these straight-leg jeans apart from others on this list is the affordable price tag, the unbelievable stretch of the denim, and the custom inseam length options for petite and tall people. This style comes in sizes 16 through 30 with three inseam options; Petite has a 28" inseam, Medium has a 30" inseam, and Long has a 32" inseam. 

    Petite shoppers can feel confident in this select as many of the 4-5 star reviews from verified Amazon shoppers praise these jeans for their amazing fit and comfort on the body.

    Best tall: m jeans by maurices™ Classic Straight Curvy High Rise Jean

    Check price at Maurice

    • Size range: 14W-24W

    TikTok has turned into a wonderful source for plus-size babes to see clothes options on actual fat people. That results in the resurgence of popular brands like Maurice's, a historically known plus-size friendly shop. They don't disappoint in the jean category with these stylish curvy high-rise pants that fit comfortably on the waist and have impressive inseam options for various heights. 

    The cotton, polyester, and Spandex blend give the jeans a stretch that holds up over time and through the wash. If you're a member of the tall-girl/tall-person club and over too-short jeans, these ones from maurices come in a long (34" inches) and extra long inseam (36' inches) option, meaning you'll never have to worry about accidentally wearing high waters ever again.

    Best stretchy: Spanx Ankle Skinny Jeans

    Check price at Spanx

    • Size range: XS-3X

    Spanx sells jeans and they're freaking AMAZING. When you're in need of anything stretchy, look no further than the brand behind the ultimate stretch. They now sell some of the best plus-size jeans that come in regular, petite, and tall options. 

    These skinny jeans are similar to jeggings with more durability thanks to the double lining design made with polyester and cotton that makes them breathable. Oh, and there are functional back pockets! When putting these Spanx on for the first time you might feel some resistance over your stomach, but after a good pull, the material stretches without losing the denim look, and keeps you secure with the shaping technology.  

    See our review of the Spanx Perfect Pant collection here.

    Best rigid: Madewell Plus Baggy Straight Jeans

    Check price at Madewell

    • Size range: 14W-28W

    Embrace your inner 90's kid that you used to be or have always wanted to be with these baggy straight-leg jeans from Madewell. The old-school rigid denim style is trendy with Gen Z for a reason and it has everything to do with the comfort of a slouchy 100% cotton feel combined with the pretty light wash shade that makes dressing fashionably easier. 

    Keep in mind that these jeans have no stretch to them so it's important to size correctly, or size up if you like an oversized fit. When I tried these, I sized up to a 26W from my usual 24W to fit better with my extra-wide stomach so I didn't lose any length with the 30" inseam. Smith also jumped on Madewell's selections when she noticed their diverse-size guide. "Madewell's jeans size chart is the first time I've exactly matched specific jean measurements for waist and hip, so I had to try them." Complete the full 90's look with your favorite vintage band tee or an option from Hot Topic.

    Best mom jeans: Abercrombie Curve Love High Rise Mom Jean

    Check price at Abercrombie

    • Size range: 23W-37W

    Plus-size content creator, Jorden Hall, took the task of finding plus-size friendly jeans very seriously and created a whole series on her TikTok where she tried 15 pairs of jeans from various brands (many that are included in this list). 

    She shared with Insider that her favorite jeans at the moment are the Abercrombie & Fitch Curve Love Jeans for their stylish fit. "You have to order them online because it is very rare to have plus sizes in store. However, I do think that these jeans are a great option for plus size if you can get your hands on a pair!" These mom jeans come in four washes, a light, medium, dark, and black option—so who's to say you can't buy all of them and build your denim collection for endless outfit options? A&F has a seemingly confusing size guide because they don't separate their plus-size and straight-size options—meaning their 37W is actually a "traditional" plus-size 24W.

    Best boyfriend jeans: Molly & Isadora Acela Boyfriend Jean

    Check price at Dia

    • Size range: 10-32

    Fashion history tells us that the ever-popular boyfriend jeans were first popularized by the one and only Marilyn Monroe, when she wore a pair on the set of the 1961 film, "Misfits." Over 60 years later the boyfriend jean is an often sought-out style for a relaxed fit that still shows off your body's natural curves. 

    You'll find that stylish fit in these jeans from Molly & Isadora at Dia & Co that features a rolled hem for added flare. Available in six different color washes there's an option for all fashion types, whether you're into light wash jeans to pair with a blouse or a dark shade to throw a leather jacket over. If you're a little taller, these bottoms are best paired with a boot or high-top sneaker to offset the smaller inseam (29") that might fall short on those with more height.

    Best distressed jeans: Eloquii Yvette Rigid Wide Jeans

    Check price at Eloquii
    • Size range: 14-28

    It seems like distressed/ripped jeans came into style and never went away. It's one of those closet staples that everyone from Hailey Bieber to Lizzo has been seen wearing and it's easy to see why. The intentional tearing gives these jeans a relaxed, cool girl vibe.  The denim itself is also made for subtle softness with materials like cotton, recycled cotton, polyester, and rayon materials woven together to create a non-stretch fit. Just watch the compliments roll in when you wear these medium-light wash jeans to a date night with a sparkly top or in the office with an oversized blazer.

    Best white jeans: Universal Thread Women's High-Rise 90's Vintage Straight Jeans

    Check price at Target

    • Size range: 00-30

    Another favorite for best plus-size jeans comes from the Target brand, Universal Thread. "My favorite part of [Universal Thread's] jeans is how accessible they are. You can find your size in-store and online. They hold up in shape, have many different style options, and truly flatter a plus-size body. I love the great quality you get for such a good price. Universal Thread is always a brand I will recommend to my plus-size girls!" Ignore all the old sayings and wear these 90's vintage straight-leg jeans whenever you want, yes even after labor day. Just make sure you have a stain remover system ready to go.

    Best black jeans: H&M+ 90's Straight High Jeans

    Check price at H&M

    • Size range: 12-26

    Everything is better in black. Black lipstick, black boots, black nail polish, and of course, black jeans! These 90s vintage style jeans from H&M+ prove my point with a dark shade option that keeps its hue even after several washes. I have been semi-skeptical of H&M as a brand ever since they removed in-store plus-size options in 2016. However, they've made me a believer again with these durable jeans that won't thin out in the thighs (even after month-long wear) thanks to the thick cotton and inner polyester lining. If you're between sizes, consider sizing up (if possible) for extra needed space in the waist.

    Best pull-on jeans: Good American Power Stretch Pull-On Skinny Jeans

    Check price at Good American
    • Size range: XS-5XL

    Everyone should have a pair of no-fuss pull-on jeans that make your butt and legs look good without the suffocating compression of jegging-like pants. That's where Good American comes in with their iconic body sculpting pants that keep the denim look in a medium dark blue wash. Insider put these jeans to the test with different body types and all three editors were amazed by the quality, Lauren Savoie, the deputy editor for Insider Reviews, said, "This was my first experience with Good American jeans, and I was surprised how quickly they became my favorite pair in my closet. My life uniform is black skinny jeans; I've tried a lot of different pairs, and this one is by far the comfiest and best fitting."

    Check out our review of Good American's pull-on jeans to see why they're one of the best plus-size jeans for any body type. 

    Best straight-leg jeans: Levi Ribcage Straight Ankle

    Check price at Amazon
    Check price at Nordstrom
    Check price at Macys

    • Size range: 14M-24L

    When talking about jeans you know Levi's is going to be brought into the conversation since they're literally the inventors of blue jeans. Over 100 years later and the brand is still at it making quality jeans for a diverse range of bodies. These ultra hi-rise jeans are made from stretchy denim to work with your body while showing off your figure. Sam Milholland, a plus-size style digital creator, says that the Levi Ribcage Straight Ankle are some of the best plus-size jeans because of their fit around her belly and waist.

    "I often have issues with jeans fitting well everywhere but then gapping at the waist since I have a 12-inch difference between my waist and hips. These jeans also have a high rise, so they actually hit my natural waist. I also love that they are a classic style and I can wear them with literally anything and they look good." Since they're ankle jeans the length might fall a little short on taller folks, but that's nothing a cute pair of platforms can't fix.

    Best skinny jeans: Lane Bryant Signature Fit Body Icon Skinny Jean

    Check price at Lane Bryant

    • Size range: 22-34/36

    Skinny jeans have a tight grip on the plus-size community because some random designer somewhere decided what we needed was girdle-like technology to "hide" ourselves. That's not what skinny jeans mean today and these pants from Lane Bryant confirm that. Iconic is in the name of these jeans and that's for a reason. The curve-centric fit offers a stretchy waistband to provide more comfort and fewer waistband lines dented on your stomach.

    According to Brunialti, "We do not scale our garments for size—the jeans we offer are truly designed for plus size bodies." This means they take into consideration larger stomachs, butts, thighs, etc. — making them one of the best plus-size jeans you can buy.

    Made from recycled plastic bottles, Lane Bryant offers an eco-friendly option that isn't made from excessive Spandex so your body is more supported not compressed. Also, you won't need someone to peel you out of them when it's time to swap your jeans out for sweats.

    Best for big thighs: WIT & WISDOM 'Itty Bitty' Bootcut Jeans

    Check price at Nordstorm

    • Size range: 14W-24W

    Sure, "Thick Thighs Save Lives" or whatever people online keep saying, but thick thighs do not keep my pants from shredding apart with every step I take. You can eliminate that fear with these bootcut jeans from Wit & Wisdom which are made from durable cotton that can withstand long-time wear and any form of movement. Another brand recommended by Nordstrom Stylist, Di Ciaula for fat folks. A first-time shopper of these jeans loves the on-trend and comfortable style and said, "I have been trying to find the best plus-size jeans for months now, and I finally found them!" Wit & Wisdom offers these jeans in sizes 14W through 24W for a dressed-up look with the added comfort of the cotton and polyester blend. The 20" ankle opening gives wider legs extra space to wear boots underneath without any tightness on the body.

    Best designer jeans: Tommy Hilfiger Plus Size Waverly Sateen Jeans

    Check price at Macy's

    • Size range: 14W-24W

    Designer jeans can mean different things to different people. Sometimes it's a pair of jeans that has a high price tag because of the materials used and an expected higher quality, and sometimes it's just because a major designer made them. These jeans from Tommy Hilfiger offer the best parts of both with an added bonus of a mid-range price point. The Waverly Sateen style is made with intricate detailing, gold hardware, and the traditional dark blue, red, and white coloring synonymous with the brand. The cotton, polyester, and subtle elastane material molds to the body with an inner "tummy control" panel that leaves a smooth and seamless look around the stomach.

    Best light wash: Old Navy High-Waisted OG Straight Button-Fly Extra-Stretch Jeans

    Check price at Old Navy

    • Size range: 00-30

    Old Navy has an impressive range of some of the best plus-size jeans. You really can't go wrong with any of their styles, as long as you know your size. The popular retailer offers styles in 00 to 30 with a true-to-size fit, however, most plus-size folks who've tried their jeans recommend sizing up. Once you've got your size and inseam selected, you can enjoy these high-waisted extra-stretch jeans in a stunning light blue wash.

    Pair these bottoms with an equally light-colored shirt for a spring-day look worthy of a first date or picnics in the park with friends. Extra bonus points to Old Navy for including recycled lycra in the material makeup to give pants an added stretch that plus-size folks will gravitate towards.

    Best medium wash: Roaman's Women's Plus Size Wide-Leg Jean

    Check price at Walmart
    Check price at Roamans

    • Size range: 12W-44W

    Flip through any magazine, celebrity Instagram page, or pants rack at a thrift store and you'll find a pair of medium-wash jeans—it's a classic for a reason. If you need an easy style option that'll work with your existing wardrobe, look no further than these wide-leg jeans from Roaman. 

    Many reviewers on Amazon were raving about these jeans for their bootcut fit that isn't tight on the thighs or the waist. With hundreds of 4-star reviews, the main takeaway is the stretch of the pants that adds extra room and comfort. The soft-to-the-touch denim is offered in petite, plus-size, and tall options, making it a truly size-inclusive pick. An invisible stretch technology is added to these jeans with the durability of cotton to keep the beloved denim look without causing discomfort in the waist, butt, and thighs.

    What to Look for in Plus-Size Jeans?

    When selecting the best size for yourself, Di Ciaula recommends measuring for your desired "rise" look. "If you are going with low-rise jeans you would measure your hips, for mid-rise measure right below the belly button, and for high-rise, measure a bit above the belly button!" If you can't be bothered to measure yourself or just don't have the time, most brands featured in this guide use True Fit sizing to best determine your fit based on clothes you already own.

    High-rise jeans, also known as high-waisted jeans, are a best friend to plus-size folks who want a long waistband that fits snugly over wide stomachs without annoying folding or rolling. Smith seconds this and shared with Insider, "I think everyone should choose a rise that they enjoy, but for me, I've been a long-time fan of the security and fit of high-rise jeans on my body, so I'm always looking for that."

    When it comes to the numerical and alphabetical portion of jean size, keep in mind that the W next to most plus-size jeans refers to it being in women's jeans sizing. Murphy explains, "It means that the clothing is cut with a looser, more relaxed fit. The brands that use women's sizing are often made specifically for those with more generous curves."

    Once you've got the right size for you, you're ready to jump into the pool of options for plus-size jeans. Taking inspiration from plus-size influencers, celebrities, and stylists check out these 19 best plus-size jeans that'll work for all types of fat folks—from petite-plus to curvy to tall.
